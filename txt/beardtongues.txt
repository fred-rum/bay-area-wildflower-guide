==bunchleaf penstemon:P. heterophyllus
color: purple
. <b>anther sacs 2.2--3 mm, dehiscing at proximal end to 1/2--2/3 their length, inner margins long-hairy</b>
.. for all others, anther sacs dehiscing full length, generally spreading widely apart; anthers glabrous
. inflorescence +- glabrous
. corolla 23--40 mm
. staminode glabrous
. common

==scarlet bugler:P. centranthifolius
. inflorescence glabrous
. <b>flower red</b>
.. all others purple-shaded
. staminode glabrous
. leaf thick, entire
. uncommon

==sierra beardtongue:P. heterodoxus var. heterodoxus
. inflorescence glandular
. <b>corolla 10--16 mm</b>
. staminode moderately yellow-hairy
. leaf entire, sometimes folded lengthwise
. extremely rare on Mt. Diablo

==rattan's beardtongue:P. rattanii
. inflorescence glandular
. corolla 20--30 mm
. staminode yellow-hairy
. <b>leaf thin, shallowly toothed</b>
. uncommon in the Santa Cruz range

==grinnell's beardtongue:P. grinnellii var. scrophularioides
. inflorescence glandular-hairy
. corolla 26--35 mm
. staminode exserted, densely yellow- or golden-hairy
. <b>leaf thick, generally folded lengthwise and arching</b>, at least partially dentate
. very rare
