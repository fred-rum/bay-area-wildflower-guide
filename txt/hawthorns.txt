==common hawthorn:C. monogyna
color:white
. leaves are deeply lobed, sometimes almost to the midrib
. flower has 1 style
. fruit is round and has 1 seed

==midland hawthorn:Crataegus laevigata
. leaves are shallowly lobed with forward-pointing lobes
. flower has > 1 style
. fruit is somewhat oval in cross section and has > 1 seed
