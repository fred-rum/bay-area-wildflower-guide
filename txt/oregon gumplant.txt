==coastal gum plant:var. platyphylla
. up to 100 cm tall
. herb; <b>stems woody for <= 10 cm</b> at base of plant
. <b>phyllaries generally spreading</b>, recurved, or coiled, round in cross section
. leaves generally sessile, sometimes clasping
. found in all coastal biomes

==marsh gumplant:var. angustifolia
. 100-200 cm tall
. subshrub; <b>stems woody for 30-150 cm</b> at base of plant
. <b>phyllaries appressed except for erect tip</b> < 3 mm, flat in cross section
. leaves at least sometimes clasping
. found in tidal wetlands
