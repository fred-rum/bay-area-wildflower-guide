com:california gilia

==california gilia:ssp. achilleifolia
color: purple
. 8--25 flowers in hemispheric head
.. corolla 10--21 mm long (2--3 &times; calyx)
.. pedicel 1--2 mm

==many-stemmed gilia:ssp. multicaulis # common name from Calflora
color: purple, pale purple, white
acom: small california gilia # Pine Ridge Association
acom: many-stemmed california gilia # Calflora
. 1--7 flowers in open cyme
.. corolla 5--10 mm long (1--2 &times; calyx)
.. pedicel 1--30 mm
. when the flower is very pale, it may be mistaken for chaparral gilia (<i>Gilia angelensis</i>), but that doesn't grow in the bay area
