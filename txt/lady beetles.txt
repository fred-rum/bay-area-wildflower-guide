sci:family Coccinellidae

Lady beetles can usually be distinguished by the pattern of dark spots
on the carapace and the pattern of white markings on the none#head.

==convergent lady beetle:Hippodamia convergens
bug:8374
. spots may be distinct, indistinct, or absent

==california lady beetle:Coccinella californica
bug:65466
. no spots

==seven-spotted lady beetle:Coccinella septempunctata
bug:3543
. spots are diagnostic

==asian lady beetle:Harmonia axyridis
bug:397
