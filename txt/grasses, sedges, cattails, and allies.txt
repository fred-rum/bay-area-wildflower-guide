ohioplants.org has more <a href="https://ohioplants.org/families-cyperaceae/">distinguishing information</a> for grasses vs. sedges.

==grasses:family Poaceae
. stem has a round cross section
. leaf sheath forms a complete cylinder around the stem

==sedges:family Cyperaceae
. stem has a triangular cross section
. leaf sheath clasps the stem with sheath sides slightly overlapping opposite the side with the leaf blade
.. "sedges have edges"
