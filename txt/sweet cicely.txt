==mountain sweet cicely:O. berteroi
color:white
. no involucel or vestigial
. corolla white or purple (or rarely green-white)
. leaf is 2-ternate, but the terminal leaflet may be so strongly divided that the leaf appears to be ternate-pinnate

==california sweet cicely:O. brachypoda
color:other
. involucel conspicuous
. corolla +- green-yellow
. leaf is 2--3-ternate, but the terminal leaflet may be so strongly divided that the leaf appears to be ternate-pinnate
