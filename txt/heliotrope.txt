==alkali heliotrope:H. curassavicum
color: white, purple
. stem prostrate to +- ascending, glabrous
. calyx lobes glabrous
. corolla white, <b>throat generally purple or yellow</b>
. scattered everywhere

==european heliotrope:H. europaeum
. stem ascending to erect, <b>puberulent to short-soft-hairy</b>
. calyx lobes bristly
. corolla <b>white</b>
. rare along the Pacific coast
