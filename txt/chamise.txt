The preferred pronunciation of the 'ch' is as 'sh'.

==common chamise:var. fasciculatum
color: white
. plant tall with erect/ascending branches
. twigs glabrous
. leaves 5--13 mm long
. found in the bay area

==southern chamise:var. obtusifolium
. plant tall with erect/ascending branches
. twigs hairy
. leaves 2--6.5 mm long
. <b>found only in southern California</b>

==carpet-forming chamise:var. prostratum
. plant low, mounded with decumbent branches
. <b>found only on islands and beaches of southern California</b>
