==green lynx spider:P. viridans
bug:2032
. chevron pattern along the dorsal ridge of the abdomen [may be subtle]
. includes white marks as part of the chevron
. legs are longer in proportion to body length
. longer, less rounded abdomen
. may turn reddish in cool weather
. more common

==lesser green lynx spider:P. longipalpis
bug:50658
. lacks a chevron pattern
. generally lacks white spots, bars and longitudinal white stripes, although white markings are present in some specimens
. legs are shorter in proportion to body length
. shorter, more rounded abdomen
. less common
