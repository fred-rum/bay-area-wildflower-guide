<h3>Single-color gilias</h3>

==california gilia:G. achilleifolia
. flower head <b>hemispheric or 1--7 loose flowers</b>
. corolla 5--21 mm, purple to white

==bluehead gilia:G. capitata
https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0413+1293
. flower head <b>spheric with 25--100</b> flowers
. corolla 6--13 mm, pale blue-violet

<h3>Multi-color gilias</h3>

==bird's-eye gilia:G. tricolor
color: pale purple
. many branches from base, spreading
. open clusters with 2--5 flowers
. corolla <b>10--19 mm</b>, blue-violet at tip, paler below
. corolla throat yellow or orange with large purple spots, possibly merging into a ring

==purplespot gilia:G. clivorum
color: purple
xx: any
. generally many branches, spreading or <b>erect</b>
. open clusters with 2--5 flowers
. corolla <b>6--8 mm</b>, purple to white (may fade to white near throat)
. corolla throat yellow with large purple spots
