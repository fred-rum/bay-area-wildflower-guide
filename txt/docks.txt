sci:Rumex spp.
x:hist/rare

Key features:
. arrangement of stems (from base or middle and/or stems among inflorescences)
. shape and length of leaf
. length/width of inner perianth lobe, dentate or not

==sheep's sorrel:R. acetosella
color: other, red
. [easily recognized by its strong red hue]
. <b>leaf blade hastate (spearhead-shaped) or sagittate (arrowhead-shaped) or base tapered</b>
.. (other Rumex species are not hastate or sagittate (but may have a tapered base)
. several stems from base
. no perianth tubercles
. very common

[
<h3>stem generally +- erect<br>
generally unbranched below inflorescence (inflorescence may be branched, however) (<a href="http://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/eflora/eflora_display.php?tid=42386">example illustration</a>)</h3>
Leaves may be intermixed with flowers.

[
Inner perianth lobe margin generally entire (if toothed or notched, teeth or notches < 0.3 mm)

==curly dock:R. crispus
color: other, red
. inner perianth lobe margin generally entire (if toothed or notched, teeth or notches < 0.3 mm)
. inflorescence generally dense
. <b>pedicels (3)4--8 mm</b>
. <b>inner perianth lobes 3.5--6 mm, 3--5 mm wide</b>
. <b>tubercles generally 3</b>, at least 1 larger, > 1 mm wide, but all <b>generally << 1/3 inner perianth lobe width</b> [<a href="http://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/eflora/eflora_display.php?tid=42386">illustration</a>]
. <b>leaf margin strongly wavy, especially near base</b>
. very common

==clustered dock:R. conglomeratus
color: other, red
. inner perianth lobe margin generally entire (if toothed or notched, teeth or notches < 0.3 mm)
. inflorescence +- open (between whorls)
. <b>pedicels 1--4(5) mm, generally +- >= inner perianth</b>
.. flowers are "clustered" close to the stem
. <b>inner perianth lobes 2--3 mm, 1--1.6(2) mm wide</b>
. <b>tubercles 3, +- equal, almost as wide as lobe</b> [<a href="http://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/eflora/eflora_display.php?tid=42386">illustration</a>]
. <b>leaf margin +- flat</b>
. common

]
[
Inner perianth lobe margin variously dentate, at least some teeth >= 0.3 mm [<a href="http://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/eflora/eflora_display.php?tid=42419">illustration</a>]

==fiddle dock:R. pulcher
rep: ,7
color: other, red
. [easily recognized by the chaotically spreading branches of its inflorescence]
. perennial
. stem: erect to arching at tip, 2--6(7) dm, branched, slender
. leaf: blade 4--10(15) cm, (2)3--5 cm wide
. inflorescence: <b>branches spreading</b>
. pedicels: 2--5(6) mm, thick
. inner perianth lobes 3--6 mm, 2--3 mm wide except teeth, margin generally dentate (+- entire) with 2--5(9) teeth, 0.3--2.5 mm, generally on both sides [<a href="http://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/eflora/eflora_display.php?tid=42419">illustration</a>]
. 3(1) tubercles, generally warty
. common

==broad-leaved dock:R. obtusifolius
color: other, red
. perennial
. stem: 6--12(15) dm, generally branched
. leaf: blade 20--40 cm, 10--15 cm wide, base generally cordate, margin generally entire, flat
. inflorescence: branches generally ascending
. pedicels: 2.5--8.5(10) mm, thin
. inner perianth lobe 3--6 mm, 2--3.5 mm wide except teeth, margin generally dentate with 2--5 teeth, 0.5--1.8 mm
. 3 tubercles, 1 larger (2 may be inconspicuous/absent), smooth
. scattered
