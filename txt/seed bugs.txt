sci:family Lygaeidae

==small milkweed bug:Lygaeus kalmii
bug:460
. dark gray to black with broad orange or red band on forewing, forming an "X" shape that doesn't quite meet in the middle
. head black with dull red spot on top, sometimes extending down onto face
. pronotum with red transverse band, bordered anteriorly by two black spots, and posteriorly by two black semicircular lobes

==large milkweed bug:Oncopeltus fasciatus
bug:504
. orange (or dark yellow) back with black band across middle, black diamond covering posterier, black diamond covering most of anterior (including pronotum), but with an orange border
