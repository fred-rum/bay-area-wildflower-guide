==rough cocklebur:X. strumarium
color: other
. no spines at nodes

==spiny cocklebur:X. spinosum
. golden spines at nodes, 15--30+ mm long

==californian bur:X. orientale
. has been occasionally observed in California on iNaturalist
. but is unknown to Calflora, Jepson eFlora, and Flora of North America
. <i>Xanthium</i> in general is of disputed origin, so this species may be caught up in that and/or may have bogus iNaturalist observations
