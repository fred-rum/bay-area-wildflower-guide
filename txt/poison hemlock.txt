sci:Conium maculatum
color: white

genus: n/a
x:ca

. leaf is large, 2--3-pinnately compound, with ultimate leaflets that are divided deeply
. stem has purple stripes or splotches (sometimes called the "blood of Socrates")

The hemlock conifer (a.k.a. Tsuga), a North American native, is named after the poison hemlock for unclear reasons, possibly because the crushed foliage is perceived to smell similarly.
