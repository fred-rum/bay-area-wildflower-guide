color: pink, red purple, other

==white-veined wintergreen:P. picta
color: pink, red purple, cream, white
. individual flower bract length <= pedicel
.. photos also indicate that the bract is wider at the base and distinctly cupped around the pedicel
. leaf blades generally present, 2.5--7 cm, dark green with white mottling on veins adaxially
. rare

==bog wintergreen:P. asarifolia
. individual linear flower bract length >> pedicel
. leaf blades well developed, adaxially +- shiny
. extremely rare; Big Basin
