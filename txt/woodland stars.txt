==hillside woodland star:L. heterophyllum
color: white
. hypanthium base truncate
. petals with 3 lobes

==bolander's woodland star:L. bolanderi
. hypanthium base obtusely tapered or hemispheric <a href="https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0421+1692">[example]</a>
. petals entire or with > 3 lobes
. very rare: east bay

==san francisco woodland-star:L. affine
color: white
. hypanthium tapered to pedicel [example,2]
. basal leaves lobed +- <= 50% to base
. most proximal cauline leaf 3-lobed
. petals white

==pink woodland star:L. parviflorum var. parviflorum
xx:ba
species: smallflower woodland star
. basal leaves lobed > &frac34; to base to 3-parted
. most proximal cauline leaf 3-parted
. petals white or pink
. very rare
