[
<h3>leaves opposite only near base, alternate or clustered distally<br>
proximal stem generally peeling</h3>

These are generally distinguished in Jepson by seed characteristics.  I've sorted them by maximum height (which roughly corresponds to maximum petal length).  Good luck!

==panicled willowherb:E. brachycarpum
color: pale purple
. 20--200 cm tall
. leaf 10--55 mm, linear to narrowly elliptic, generally folded along midrib, +- glabrous; petiole 0--4 mm
. panicle or raceme inflorescence
. hypanthium 1.5--8(16) mm
. petals 2--15(20) mm, white to rose-purple
. common
. see also the 5-petaled {-stiff velezia}

==denseflower willowherb:E. densiflorum
color: pale purple
. 5--150 cm tall, simple or branched distally
. leaf 14--85 mm, lanceolate to sublinear, +- sessile; distal leaves hairy
. inflorescence leafy, +- dense spike
. hypanthium 1.3--4 mm
. petals 3--10 mm, rose-purple to white
. common

==torrey's willowherb:E. torreyi
. 4--65 cm tall
. leaf 5--45 mm, +- lance-linear, hairy, +- sessile
. bracts leaf-like
. hypanthium 0.4--1 mm
. petals 1--3.2 mm, pink or white
. Jasper Ridge, Nisene Marks, Sunol, Mt. Diablo, Mt. Tam

==pygmy willowherb:E. campestre
. 10--55 cm tall
. leaf 8--35 mm, lanceolate, generally hairy, +- sessile
. inflorescence crowded
. hypanthium 0.3--1.1 mm
. petals 1--3.2 mm, pink
. Los Trancos, Don Edwards, Sunol, Pleasanton Ridge, Mt. Diablo, Briones

==california willowherb:E. ravenii
sci_fpj:E. foliosum
. < 45 cm tall
. leaf 5--30 mm, sublinear to lanceolate, generally folded along midrib, distal clustered; petiole 1--12 mm
. inflorescence crowded
. hypanthium 0.4--0.8 mm
. petals 1.8--3 mm, white
. Mt. Tam

==little willowherb:E. minutum
. < 40 cm tall
. leaf 9--25 mm, generally lanceolate to narrowly elliptic, +- glabrous; petiole 0--2 mm
. inflorescence open
. hypanthium 1.1--1.5 mm
. petals 2--5 mm, white or pink
. scattered

==selfing willowherb:E. cleistogamum
. 15--32 cm tall
. leaf 15--55 mm, linear to narrowly elliptic; distal leaves hairy, often folded along midrib
. infloescence leafy, spreading and glandular hairy
. hypanthium 0.5--1 mm
. petals 2--5.5 mm, white to pale pink
. very rare: Henry Coe
