==wild strawberry:F. vesca
color:white
. leaf thin, not leathery; central leaflet stalk 1--3 mm
. petals 4--9 mm

==beach strawberry:F. chiloensis
. leaf thick, leathery; central leaflet stalk 1--10 mm
. petals 10--18 mm
. mostly near the Pacific coast, with a few scattered around the edges of the bay
