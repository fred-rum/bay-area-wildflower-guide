Edibility: All parts of honeysuckles are generally poisonous unless specifically reported safe for a particular species. ^https://www.eattheweeds.com/tag/lonicera-hispidula/ For pink honeysuckle, "the berries are edible, but reportedly quite bitter." ^https://curbstonevalley.com/california-honeysuckle/ Note that the nector is <b>not</b> reported to be edible.
