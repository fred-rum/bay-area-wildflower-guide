sci:order Odonata

==dragonflies
. very large eyes with no gap between them
. thicker body
. wings at rest held out perpendicular to the body
. hind wings are broader at the baes and so noticeably larger than the fore wings

==damselflies
. smaller eyes with at least a little gap between them
. twig-like body
. wings at rest folded together and parallel to the body
. hind wings and fore wings the same and tapering to the body connection
