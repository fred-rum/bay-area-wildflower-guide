==california yampah:P. californica
color: white, other
. roots tuberous
. basal petiole 2--6 cm
. terminal leaf segments unlike lateral segments in size and form

==gairdner's yampah:P. gairdneri
acom: common yampah

. roots tuberous
. basal petiole 8--15 cm
. cauline leaves 1--2-pinnate or 1--2-ternate [not ternate-pinnate?]

==oregon yampah:P. oregana
. roots tuberous
. basal petiole 2--10 cm
. all leaf segments alike in size and form
