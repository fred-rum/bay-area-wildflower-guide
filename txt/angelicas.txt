==henderson's angelica:A. hendersonii
. <b>basal leaves 2--3-ternate-pinnate</b>
. <b>leaflets bright green adaxially</b>, double-serrate to crenate
. common along the pacific coast

==california angelica:A. californica
. <b>basal leaves 1-ternate-pinnate</b>
. plant glabrous to sparsely hairy
. leaflets sharply serrate
. common east of the bay and in Marin County

==woolly angelica:A. tomentosa
. <b>basal leaves 2--3-ternate-pinnate</b>
. <b>plant glaucous everywhere</b>
. leaflets serrate to entire
. less common, but can be found anywhere
