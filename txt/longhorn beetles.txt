==flower longhorn beetles

==Molorchus longicollis
bug:221909
Per Ken-ichi Ueda's <a href="https://www.inaturalist.org/observations/637591">observation</a>, there are only two species of Molorchus in California, and M. eburneus can be distinguished by the <a href="https://bugguide.net/node/view/888019/bgimage">white markings</a> on its elytra.
