==small melilot:M. indicus
color: yellow
. corolla <b>2.5--3 mm</b>, yellow
. <b>inflorescence axis generally 1--2 cm when flowers open</b>
. stem 10--60 cm
. corolla length is +- 1&frac12;--2&frac12; &times; calyx length
. wings length +- = banner length
. wings and banner length +- >= keel length
. leaflets 1--2.5 cm, oblanceolate to wedge-shaped-obovate, +- toothed
. fruit 2--3 mm, bumpy or with faint lines
. common

==yellow sweetclover:M. officinalis
. corolla 4.5--7 mm, <b>yellow</b>
. inflorescence axis generally 3--8 cm when flowers open
. stem 50--200 cm
. corolla length is +- 3--4 &times; calyx length
. wings length +- = keel length
. wings and keel length << banner length
. leaflets 1--2.5 cm, elliptic-oblong to obovate, +- toothed
. fruit 3--5 mm, plump, irregularly cross-ridged
. uncommon

==white sweetclover:M. albus
. corolla 3.5--5 mm, <b>white</b>
. inflorescence axis generally 3--8 cm when flowers open
. stem 50--200 cm
. leaflets 1--2.5 cm, elliptic-oblong to obovate, +- toothed
. fruit 3--5 mm, with network of lines
. uncommon
