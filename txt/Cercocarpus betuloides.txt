==birchleaf mountain mahogany:var. betuloides
color: other
. leaf blade generally 10--27 mm long, < 15 mm wide
. found in the bay area

==island mountain mahogany:var. blancheae
. leaf blade 27--70 mm long, > 15 mm wide
. <b>found only in southern California</b>

Calflora and Jepson also include a var. macrourus, but POWO and iNaturalist lumps this into var. betuloides.  In any case, it is not found in the bay area.
