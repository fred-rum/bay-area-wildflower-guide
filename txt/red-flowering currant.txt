sci:Ribes sanguineum
xx:any

==blood currant:R. sanguineum var. glutinosum
color: pink, pale purple
. sepals are pink to white
. drooping inflorescence
. common in the bay area

==winter currant:R. sanguineum var. sanguineum
. sepals are red
. erect or spreading inflorescence
. <b>rare to non-existent in the bay area</b>

Edibility: Ripe blue-black berry "does not have a wonderful flavour, but is tolerable raw." ^https://pfaf.org/user/Plant.aspx?LatinName=Ribes+sanguineum "Palatable but insipid" ^http://floranorthamerica.org/Ribes_sanguineum
