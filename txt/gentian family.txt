sci:family Gentianaceae

==Zeltnera
. 2 stigmas, each wedge- or fan-shaped (or 1 stigma with 2 lobes)
. stem generally branched below inflorescence

.. [except {-Zeltnera muehlenbergii}]

==Centaurium
. 2 stigmas, each elliptic to ovate
. <b>calyx lobes not keeled</b>
. stem simple below inflorescence
