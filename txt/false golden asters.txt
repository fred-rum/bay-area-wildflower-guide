"Real" golden asters (genus Chrysopsis) are generally found in the SE US.

==sessileflower false goldenaster:H. sessiliflora
. 4--24 ray flowers, generally 7--15 mm
. proximal leaves subsessile
. distal leaves sessile, reduced

==telegraphweed:H. grandiflora
. 25--40 ray flowers, generally 5--8 mm
. proximal leaves petioled, petiole clasping with ear-like basal lobes [<a href="https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+1009+2288">example</a>]
. distal leaves sessile, not clasping

==rayless false goldenaster:H. oregona
. no ray flowers
