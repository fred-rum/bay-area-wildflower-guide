==perennial ryegrass:L. perenne
sci_fpj:Festuca perennis
. 1 glume per spikelet (2 glumes for the uppermost spikelet)
.. spikelet sits between a hollow in the stem and the glume
. glume < rest of spikelet
. despite the name, is an annual or at best a perennial herb

==darnel:L. temulentum
sci_fpj:Festuca temulenta
. 1 glume per spikelet except 2 for uppermost spikelet
. glume >= rest of spikelet

==meadow fescue:L. pratense
