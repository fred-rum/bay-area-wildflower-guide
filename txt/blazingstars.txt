==lindley's blazingstar:Mentzelia lindleyi
color: yellow
. <b>petals 20--40 mm</b>

==bushy blazingstar:M. dispersa
. petals 2--6(8) mm
. <b>bracts do not cup the flower</b>
. outer filaments +- thread-like, not lobed

==san luis blazingstar:M. micrantha
. petals 2.5--4.5 mm
. <b>bracts generally cup the flower</b>
. outer 5 filaments widened and distally 2-lobed
