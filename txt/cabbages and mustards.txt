==field mustard:B. rapa
color: yellow
. stem 30--100 cm, simple or branched
. basal leaves pinnately lobed, lateral lobes 2--4(6) pairs, terminal lobe obovate, wavy-dentate
. middle and <b>distal cauline leaves</b> sessile, <b>base lobed, generally clasping</b>
. pedicel erect
. petals 6--11(13) mm
. flowers much like {-Brassica nigra}
. very common
. very similar to {-Rhamphospermum}
. some leaves may encircle the stem

==brown mustard:B. juncea
. stem 20--100 cm, branched distally
. basal leaves early-diciduous, 5--100 cm, lateral lobes in 1--3 pairs
. <b>distal cauline leaves</b> smaller, <b>base tapered, not lobed</b>
. <b>pedicel spreading to ascending</b>
. petals 9--13 mm
. rare

==cabbage, broccoli, and allies:B. oleracea
. stem 50--100 cm, branched distally
. <b>leaves fleshy</b>
. basal leaves pinnately lobed, 5--75 cm
. middle and distal cauline leaves sessile, base lobed or generally clasping
. petals <b>18--25 mm</b>
. scattered near the coast, e.g. Wilder Ranch
. some leaves may clasp the stem without encircling it
