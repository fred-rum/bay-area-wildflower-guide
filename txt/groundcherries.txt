sci: genus Physalis

ID tips are from the <a href="Invasive Species Compendium">https://www.cabi.org/isc/datasheet/40713</a>.

==cape gooseberry:Physalis peruviana
color: yellow
. pale/dingy yellow flowers
. densely pubescent
;Edibility: The ripe fruit has a delicious bitter-sweet flavour.  All other parts (including the calyx that wraps the fruit) are toxic. ^https://pfaf.org/user/plant.aspx?latinname=Physalis+peruviana ^https://books.google.com/books?id=m5LsWJl2cCkC

==large-flowered tomatillo:Physalis philadelphica
. bright yellow flowers [although many photos show it paler]
. only sparsely pubescent
;Edibility: The ripe fruit is edible.  All other parts (including the calyx that wraps the fruit) are toxic. ^https://pfaf.org/user/plant.aspx?latinname=Physalis+philadelphica ^https://books.google.com/books?id=m5LsWJl2cCkC
