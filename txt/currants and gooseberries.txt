sci:Ribes spp.
x:rare

Key features:
. spines on branch nodes or not
. bristles between branch nodes or not (particularly on distal twigs)
. sepal color, spread angle, and number
. anther color
. styles hairy at base or glabrous
. stamen length relative to petals
. leaf glandular on underside or not

<a href="https://montereywildflowers.com/grossulariaceae-gooseberries/">Monterey County Wildflowers</a> appears to be more accurate and useful than Jepson.

==fuchsiaflower gooseberry:R. speciosum
. spines at nodes
. internodes bristly
. <b>four sepals</b>
.. all others with spines have five sepals
. red sepals
. red petals
. very long red stamens (anthers red or other)

==oak gooseberry:R. quercetorum
. spines at nodes
. five sepals
. yellow/cream sepals and petals
. <b>anther filaments not longer than petals</b>
.. all others with spines and five sepals have anther filaments longer than petals
. rare in Diablo range

==bitter gooseberry:R. amarum
. purple-red sepals
. white petals
. purple or yellow-green anthers
. styles glabrous at base
. leaf blade abaxially glandular
. no bristles between nodes
. rare in Diablo range

. leaf blade abaxially glandular
