==western bewildering bushmallow:var. arcuatus
. bractlets subtending calyx >= 3 mm, >= &frac12; calyx length
. branches of stellate hairs on stem generally > 0.5 mm, do not require magnification to see
. peninsula

==eastern bewildering bushmallow:var. elmeri
color: pale purple, pink
. bractlets subtending calyx <= 3.5 mm, <= &frac12; calyx length
. branches of stellate hairs on stem generally <= 0.5 mm, require magnification to see
. Mt. Diablo, Diablo range, foothills around 101 south of San Jose
