==water primrose:L. hexapetala
color: yellow
. bracts lanceolate to lance-ovate
. sepals 8--19 mm
. petals 15--29 mm
. Despite the Latin name, typically has only 5 petals.

==floating primrose-willow:L. peploides
color: yellow
. bracts deltate
. sepals 6--14 mm
. petals 9--22 mm
