==hunt's clarkia:C. amoena ssp. huntiana
. ovary and immature fruit 4-grooved
. stem erect
. inflorescence open
. internode generally > subtending flower
. very rare: Mt. Tam and Milagra Ridge

==n/a:C. amoena ssp. amoena
. ovary and immature fruit 4-grooved
. stem decumbent
. inflorescence dense
. internode generally < subtending flower
. rare: but modern records are extremely rare

==whitney's clarkia:C. amoena ssp. whitneyi
acom:whitney's farewell-to-spring
. ovary and immature fruit 8-grooved
. stem decumbent
. inflorescense dense
. petals 30--60 mm
. extremely rare: in Half Moon Bay, possibly cultivated
