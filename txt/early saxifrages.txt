sci:Micranthes spp.
family:saxifrage family
x:hist/rare

==california saxifrage:M. californica
color: white
. <b>leaf blade shallowly toothed</b>
. inflorescence +- open, often 1-sided
. flowers not clustered to loosely clustered
. anthers purple to red (rarely orange)
. petal length +- 2 &times; sepals

==wholeleaf saxifrage:M. integrifolia
. <b>leaf blade +- entire to minutely toothed</b>
. inflorescence never 1-sided
. flowers loosely to densely clustered
. anthers generally yellow to orange
. petal lengths < 1.7 &times; sepals
