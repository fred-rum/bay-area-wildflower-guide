sci:Stachys spp.
x:rare

Key features:
. pouch at base of corolla or not
. inflorescence elongated or compact
. leaf hairs long & silky or not

See also the similar-looking {-lemon balm}.

==california hedge nettle:S. bullata
color: pale purple
. <b>no pouch at base of corolla</b> [example,3]
. inflorescence elongated
. leaf blade 3--18 cm, +- ovate, base +- cordate, tip obtuse; soft- and stiff-hairy, glandular
. corolla pink to +- purple

==rough hedgenettle:S. rigida
color: pale purple
. pouch at base of corolla [example,3]
. inflorescence elongated
. leaf blade 5--9 cm, ovate to lanceolate, base rounded to cordate, tip acute to obtuse; <b>glabrous to soft-hairy (not silky)</b>, occasionally glandular
. stem, herbage +- glandular, not resinous; lower corolla lip 4--6 mm wide
. corolla pink, bracts rapidly reduced distally on stem

==bugle hedgenettle:S. ajugoides
https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+1207+0830
. pouch at base of corolla
. inflorescence elongated
. leaf blade 1.5--7 cm, generally oblong, base wedge-shaped, tip rounded; <b>hairs long & silky</b>
. corolla pink or white

==short-spiked hedge nettle:S. pycnantha
https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0311+0492
. pouch at base of corolla
. <b>inflorescence compact</b>
. leaf blade 5--12 cm, ovate or lanceolate to oblong, crenate to serrate, base rounded to cordate, tip obtuse; soft- to stiff-hairy, glandular
. corolla +- white to +- pink

==coastal hedge-nettle:S. chamissonis
https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0606+1039
. corolla tube 18--24 mm
.. all others are < 15 mm
. leaf blade 6--18 cm, deltoid to ovate, soft-hairy, densely glandular abaxially
. <b>corolla deep magenta to purple</b>
