==california polypody:P. californicum
<b>Caution: ID unsure.</b>
. leaf blade deltate to ovate, often +- irregular in outline
. proximal 1--3 pinnae often at least as long as others
. sori generally +- sunken

==nested polypody:P. calirhiza
<b>Caution: ID unsure.</b>
. leaf blade oblong-ovate, +- regular in outline
. proximal 1--3 pinnae generally shorter than others
. sori not sunken
