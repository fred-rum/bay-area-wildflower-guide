==oregon grape:B. aquifolium

. compound leaf with <b>petiole > 3 cm</b>
.. usually 5--9 leaflets, +- pinnately veined
... leaflet +- <b>flat</b>, margin <b>+- thin, not hard</b>
... 12-24 spines per side

==shining netvein barberry:Berberis dictyota

... leaflet margin strongly wavy, <b>thick, usually hard</b>
... 6--10 spines per side, 2--5 mm long
