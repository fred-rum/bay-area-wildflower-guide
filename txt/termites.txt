==western drywood termite:Incisitermes minor
bug:469117
. head and pronotum are orange brown
. pronotum is rectangular and at least as wide as the head
. abdomen is dark brown
. front wing has 3 dark, hardened veins in the front portion

==pacific dampwood termite:Zootermopsis angusticollis
bug:75929
. head, pronotum, and abdoment are all light to medium brown
. pronotum is crescent shaped and less wide than the head
. wings are dark brown, thinner than in {-western drywood termite}
