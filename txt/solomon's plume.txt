A key to subspecies can be found in <a href="http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1&taxon_id=242101759">Flora of North America</a>.

==western solomon's plume:ssp. amplexicaule
color: white
sci_fj: n/a
. the only subspecies in California
. stem erect
. leaf sessile, clasping
.. blade base rounded
.. tip of third leaf below inflorescence acute

==eastern solomon's plume:ssp. racemosum
sci_fpj: n/a
. found only in the eastern US
. stem arching
. leaf with [very short] petiole
.. blade base tapered
.. third leaf below inflorescence tapering into a thin tip, 12--25 mm long
