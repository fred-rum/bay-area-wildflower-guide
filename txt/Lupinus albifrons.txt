==silver bush lupine:L. albifrons var. albifrons
color: purple, pale purple
. shrub 50 cm--5 m; woody +- throughout; <b>trunk is generally distinct</b>
. bract 4--8 mm

==douglas' silver lupine:L. albifrons var. douglasii
. shrub 1--2 m; woody +- throughout
. <b>bract 10--24 mm</b>

==silver lupine:L. albifrons var. collinus
color: purple
. <b>subshrub, 20--40 cm; only base and some lower branches are woody</b>
. bract 7--9 mm
