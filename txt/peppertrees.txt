==peruvian peppertree:S. molle
color: cream
. distal branches & twigs flexible, drooping
. leaflets generally > 15, generally < 1 cm wide
. rare; mostly in developed areas

==brazilian pepper:S. terebinthifolia
color: cream
. distal branches & twigs stiff, spreading to erect
. leaflets 7 (rarely 13), generally > 1 cm wide
. very rare; generally in developed areas
