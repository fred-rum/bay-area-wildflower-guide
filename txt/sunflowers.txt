==common sunflower:H. annuus
color: yellow
. phyllaries 13--25 mm long, generally 5--8 mm wide, abruptly acuminate
.. all others have phyllaries 1--5 mm wide, acute or gradually acuminate
. leaf blade abaxially bristly
. stem +- rough-hairy

==california sunflower:H. californicus
color: yellow
. phyllaries 10--25 mm long, generally 3--5 mm wide, generally >> disk
.. tips of outer phyllaries generally reflexed at maturity

==serpentine sunflower:H. exilis
sci_j:H. bolanderi
color: yellow
. phyllaries 8--27 mm long, 3--5 mm wide, often >> disk

==slender sunflower:H. gracilentus
. phyllaries generally unequal, 4--8 mm long, 1.5--3 mm wide, <= disk
