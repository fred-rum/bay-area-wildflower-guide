==johnnytuck:T. eriantha
. <b>plant +- purple</b>
. <b>corolla 10--25 mm, > bract</b>, yellow or white fading to pale purple
. beak dark purple
. <b>pouches 3--4 mm deep</b>

==purplebeak owl's-clover:T. micrantha
. <b>plant +- purple</b>
. <b>corolla 8--15 mm, +- = bract</b>, yellow
. beak dark purple
. <b>pouches 1--2 mm deep</b>

==yellowbeak owl's-clover:T. versicolor
. <b>plant green-brown to yellow-brown [i.e. greenish]</b>
. corolla 12--22 mm, yellow or white fading to pale purple
. beak +- yellow
. pouches 2--4 mm deep

==san francisco owl's-clover:T. floribunda
. plant yellow-brown [green]
. corolla 10--14 mm, cream, <b>stamens exserted</b>
.. all other stamens are included
. north peninsula

==dwarf orthocarpus:T. pusilla
. plant yellow-brown or +- purple
. <b>corolla 4--7 mm</b>, +- purple
.. all other corollas are >= 8 mm
