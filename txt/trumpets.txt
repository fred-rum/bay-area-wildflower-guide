==variableleaf collomia:C. heterophylla
color: red purple, pink, white
. <b>corolla lobes red purple or pink to white, throat white, tube greenish yellow</b>
.. corolla 10--14 mm long
. stem branching and spreading with long glandular hairs
. lower cauline leaves 1--2-pinnate lobed (upper leaves lobed or entire)
. uncommon

==serpentine collomia:C. diversifolia
. <b>corolla petals red purple to pink, throat white and yellow, tube red or brown</b>
.. corolla 9--12 mm long
. stem branching and spreading with long glandular hairs
. lower cauline leaves +-3-toothed (upper leaves entire)
. very rare, mostly on Mt. Diablo

==grand collomia:C. grandiflora
color: cream, white, other
. <b>corolla dusty orange, fading to white</b>
.. corolla 15--30 mm long
. stem erect and simple with short nonglandular hairs
. rare
