==common snowberry:S. albus
xx:uncat
color: pale purple, red purple
. plant 60--180 cm, erect
. corolla swollen on 1 side, with 5 nectary glands within swelling
. inflorescence generally 8--16-flowered
;Edibility: Contains saponins, and saponins make things taste like soap ^https://www.picturethisai.com/wiki/Symphoricarpos_mollis.html .  Sapopins are also toxic, although poorly absorbed.  It would take many kilos of fruit from this plant in order to produce toxic symptoms ^https://pfaf.org/user/Plant.aspx?LatinName=Symphoricarpos+albus .

==creeping snowberry:S. mollis
color: pale purple, red purple
. Plant 15--60 cm, sprawling
. corolla scarcely or not swollen, 5 nectary glands, 1 below each corolla lobe
. inflorescence generally 2--8-flowered
;Edibility: Contains saponins, and saponins make things taste like soap ^https://www.picturethisai.com/wiki/Symphoricarpos_mollis.html .  Sapopins are also toxic, although poorly absorbed.  It would take many kilos of fruit from this plant in order to produce toxic symptoms ^https://pfaf.org/user/Plant.aspx?LatinName=Symphoricarpos+albus .  Not to be confused with a tastier creeping snowberry, <i>Gaultheria hispidula</i>.
