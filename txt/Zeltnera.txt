There are more centauries in the {-Gentianaceae}.

<b>Caution: IDs uncertain.</b>

Key features:
. corolla eye
. maximum pedicel length
. calyx lobes keeled or not
. corolla lobe length
. stigma number and shape
. basal leaves / rosette

==muehlenberg's centaury:Z. muehlenbergii
acom:monterey centaury
color: pink, pale purple
. usually 1 stem below inflorescence, simple or branching ^https://floranorthamerica.org/Zeltnera_muehlenbergii
. pedicels <b>0</b>--10 mm
.. <b>mostly < 6 mm</b> ^http://floranorthamerica.org/Zeltnera
.. distal flowers often sessile
... [bractlets (shorter) may be confused with sepals (longer) for sessile flowers]
. <b>calyx lobes keeled</b> ^https://www.inaturalist.org/journal/rymcdaniel/111899-differentiating-zeltnera-muehlenbergii-from-centaurium-and-other-species-in-western-north-america
. corolla lobes 2--7 mm, tips rounded
.. small white eye barely protuding onto lobes
... may include projection centrally (type A) or laterally (type B) ^https://www.inaturalist.org/journal/rymcdaniel/111899-differentiating-zeltnera-muehlenbergii-from-centaurium-and-other-species-in-western-north-america
.. orifice generally spacious, pentagonal ^https://www.inaturalist.org/journal/rymcdaniel/111899-differentiating-zeltnera-muehlenbergii-from-centaurium-and-other-species-in-western-north-america
. uncommon

==davy's centaury:Z. davyi
color: pink, pale purple
. 1--10 stems, simple or few-branched ^https://floranorthamerica.org/Zeltnera_davyi
. pedicels 2--25(55) mm
.. <b>rarely < 4 mm</b> ^https://floranorthamerica.org/Zeltnera_davyi
. <b>calyx lobes keeled</b>
.. Caution: some species have paired bracts that resemble calyx lobes, but the calyx actually has the same number of lobes as the corolla (4 or 5)
. corolla lobes 3--7 mm
.. prominent white eye [extending significantly onto the lobes], rounded pentagonal or star shaped ^https://www.inaturalist.org/journal/rymcdaniel/111899-differentiating-zeltnera-muehlenbergii-from-centaurium-and-other-species-in-western-north-america
. uncommon

==alkali centaury:Z. trichantha
. [many flowered]
. <b>pedicels 0--3 mm</b>
. corolla lobes 5--10 mm, <b>tips acute to acuminate</b>
. rare

==california centaury:Z. venusta
. [much branched, many flowered]
. pedicels generally 4--25 mm
. <b>corolla lobes 5--20 mm</b>
. rare

==tall centaury:Z. exaltata
. [few to many flowered]
. <b>pedicels generally 10--70 mm, generally > closed corollas</b>
.. all others are 0--40 mm, generally < closed corollas
. <b>flower parts generally in 4s</b>
.. all others in 5s
. rare
