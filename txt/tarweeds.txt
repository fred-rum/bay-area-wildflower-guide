<h3>relatively large diameter flower head</h3>

==common madia:M. elegans
color: yellow
. stem 6--250 cm, proximally soft- to coarse-hairy, distally glandular-hairy
. leaf 3--20 cm, 2--10 mm wide, lanceolate to linear
. <b>heads in open</b>, flat-topped clusters
. involucre 4.5--12 mm, +- coarse- or soft-hairy, generally also glandular-hairy
. phyllary tips erect or reflexed, flat
. phyllaries extend partway up petal (<a href="https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+1107+1511">example</a>)
. <b>25--80 disk flowers</b>, staminate (no pistil)
. 5--22 ray flowers, 4--20 mm, <b>relatively narrow petals</b>, bright yellow, <b>often maroon at base</b>
. scattered, especially south of the bay

==showy golden madia:M. radiata
https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0810+1019
. stem 10--90 cm, glandular-hairy
. leaf 2--10 cm, 4--15 mm wide, lanceolate to linear
. <b>heads in open</b>, +- flat-topped clusters
. involucre 4--7 mm, soft-hairy to bristly, hairs hooked at tip, and glandular-hairy
. phyllary tips +- erect or reflexed, flat
. <b>18--65 disk flowers</b>, <b>anthers yellow to +- brown</b>
. 8--16 ray flowers, 6--19 mm, <b>relatively wide petals</b>, golden yellow
. La Honda Creek

<h3>relatively small diameter flower head</h3>

==lemon scented tarweed:M. citriodora
. stem 10--70 cm, proximally hairy, distally gladular-hairy, glands purple
. leaf 2--9 cm, 1--10 mm wide, linear
. heads in open, flat-topped clusters
. involucre 6--8 mm, glandular-hairy, often sparsely so
. phyllary tips generally +- erect, flat
. 8--50+ disk flower, anthers +- dark purple
. 5--12 ray flowers, 4--11 mm, green-yellow
. out of its normal range at Henry Coe

==grassy tarweed:M. gracilis
color: yellow
. stem 6--100 cm, <b>proximally soft- to coarse-hairy</b>, distally glandular-hairy
. leaf 1--10 cm, 1--8 mm wide, oblong to linear
. <b>heads in +- open</b>, panicle- or raceme-like clusters
. involucre 5--10 mm, finely or coarsely <b>glandular-hairy</b>
. phyllary tips erect or +- reflexed, flat
. 2--16+ disk flowers, anthers +- dark purple
. 3--10 ray flowers, 1.5--8 mm, lemon-yellow or green-yellow
. very common

==coast tarweed:M. sativa
color: yellow
. stem 35--100 cm, coarse- and glandular-hairy throughout
. leaf 2--18 cm, 3--<b>18 mm wide</b>, broadly lanceolate to linear-oblong or linear
. <b>heads in generally crowded</b>, panicle-, raceme-, or spike-like clusters
. involucre 6--16 mm, coarse- and <b>glandular-hairy</b>
. phyllary tips erect or +- reflexed, flat
. <b>11--45+ disk flowers</b>, anthers +- dark purple
. (5)8--13 ray flowers, 1.5--4 mm, green-yellow or occasionally purple-red abaxially or throughout
. very common

==plump seeded madia:M. anomala
. stem 20--55 cm, coarse-hairy, distally glandular-hairy
. heads in flat-topped, raceme-, or panicle-like clusters; involucre 5--10 mm; mostly away from immediate coast
. leaf 2--10 cm, 2--7 mm wide, linear
. <b>heads in open</b>,  raceme- or panicle-like clusters
. involucre 6--10 mm, <b>coarse-hairy and glandular-hairy</b>
. phyllary tips erect or +- reflexed, flat
. 3--8 disk flowers, anthers +- dark purple
. 3--8 ray flowers, 3--4.5 mm, green-yellow
. Sunol, Mt. Tam

==small tarweed:M. exigua
. stem 1--30 cm, coarse- and glandular-hairy
. leaf 0.2--4 cm, 0.5--2 mm wide, linear
. <b>heads in open</b>, flat-topped or raceme- or panicle-like clusters
. involucre 2.5--5 mm, <b>+- coarse- and glandular-hairy</b>
. phyllary tips +- erect, <b>grooved along midvein</b>
. <b>1(2) disk flower</b>, <b>anthers yellow to +- brown</b>
. 1--8 ray flowers, 0.7--1 mm, pale yellow
. scattered
