==most beautiful jewelflower:S. glandulosus ssp. glandulosus
color: purple, pale purple, red purple
. raceme not 1-sided
. sepals +- red-purple to dark maroon or lilac-lavender

==metcalf canyon jewelflower:S. glandulosus ssp. albidus
. raceme not 1-sided
. sepals white to +- green-white or pale yellow

==mount tamalpais bristly jewelflower:S. glandulosus ssp. pulchellus
. raceme 1-sided
.. photos suggest ~90&deg; between flowers (instead of 180&deg;)
. sepals lavender to rose or purple
. Marin

==one-sided jewelflower:S. glandulosus ssp. secundus
. raceme 1-sided
. sepals +- green-white, base lavender to +- purple
. Marin

==tiburon jewelflower:S. glandulosus ssp. niger
. pedicel 1--3.2 cm
.. all others 0.2--1.5 cm
. <b>sepals dark purple-black</b>
.. all others not dark purple-black, although ssp. glandulosus can be pretty close
. raceme axis wavy [subtle]
.. all others straight [not true?]
. proximal cauline leaves glabrous
.. all others sparsely to densely stiff-hairy (rarely glabrous)
. Tiburon
