==western columbine:A. formosa
color: red, salmon
. each petal opening is +-round
. <b>inner petal edge <= 90&deg; to exposed filaments</b>
. outer petal edge extended 1--8 mm to somewhat cup the filaments
. common

==van houtte's columbine:A. eximia
. each petal opening is +-elongated
. <b>inner petal edge > 90&deg; to exposed filaments</b>
. outer petal edge not extended, so filaments are fully visible from the side
. uncommon
