<h3>calyx lobes generally +- equal or lowest 2 longest, not upcurved<br>

==coastal monkeyflower:E. dentata
sci_p:Mimulus dentatus
. stolons or rhizomes present
. <b>leaf blade 15--70 mm</b>
. <b>herbage non-glandular</b>
. <b>corolla tube generally > 4 mm wide</b>
. calyx hairy
. very rare

==musk monkeyflower:E. moschata
sci_p:Erythranthe inodora|Mimulus moschatus
color: yellow
. stolons or rhizomes present
. <b>leaf blade 20--50 mm</b>
. <b>herbage glandular</b>
. <b>corolla tube 2--4 mm wide except wider at mouth</b>
. calyx glabrous to slimy-hairy
. leaf surfaces feel wet & slimy

==purple-stemmed monkeyflower:E. floribunda
sci_p:Mimulus floribundus
. neither stolons nor rhizomes present
. <b>leaf blade 8--25 mm</b>
. herbage hairy, generally +- slimy
. <b>calyx +- hairy</b>

==broadtooth monkeyflower:E. latidens
sci_p:Mimulus latidens
. neither stolons nor rhizomes present
. <b>leaf 4--25 mm</b>, proximal-most +- petioled, <b>in basal rosette</b>
. <b>calyx tube glabrous to puberulent</b>
. very rare
