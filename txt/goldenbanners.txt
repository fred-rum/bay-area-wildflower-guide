==california goldenbanner:T. californica
color: yellow
. plant 30--80 cm
. stem slender, ascending to erect

==santa ynez goldenbanner:T. macrophylla
. plant 80--250 cm
. stem stout, erect
. very rare, and only in Santa Barbara County
.. "Our study of type material indicates that this name has been erroneously applied to the more common T. californica var. californica"; Chen, 1994. [<a href="https://repositories.lib.utexas.edu/bitstream/handle/2152/31128/TaxonomyThermopsis.pdf">pdf</a>]
