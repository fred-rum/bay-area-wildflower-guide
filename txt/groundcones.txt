==california groundcone:Kopsiopsis strobilacea
color: other, purple, yellow
. inflorescence 2.5--5.5 cm diameter
. largest bracts 8--15(19) mm wide
. corolla 15--20 mm long

==small ground-cone:Kopsiopsis hookeri
. inflorescence 1.5--2.5 cm diameter
. largest bracts 6--8 mm wide
. corolla 10--15 mm long
