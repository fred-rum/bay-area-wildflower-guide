There are more things commonly called <b>ice plants</b> in the {-stone plant family}.

==slender iceplant:M. nodiflorum
color: cream
. <b>0.4--1.2 cm flower diameter</b>
. leaf +- cylindric
. white petals, aging to yellow
. found mostly on the levees around the salt ponds at the south end of the bay
. common

==crystalline ice plant:M. crystallinum
color: white, cream
. <b>1--3 cm flower diameter</b>
. leaf flat
. white petals, aging to pink
. rare
