==valley lupine:var. microcarpus
acom: shaggy haired chick lupine # name from Jepson
color: purple, pale purple, cream, white
. bracts and calyx with <b>dense</b> long spreading hairs
. flowers and fruit generally on <b>more than one side</b> of axis

==dense-flowered lupine:var. densiflorus
acom:white-whorled lupine
color: white, cream, pale purple
. bracts and calyx with <b>sparse</b> appressed or spreading hairs
. late flowers and fruit generally on <b>one side</b> of axis
