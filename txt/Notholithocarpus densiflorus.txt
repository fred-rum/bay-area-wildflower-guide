==tanoak:N. densiflorus var. densiflorus
color: cream, other
. tree up to 100 feet tall (but can be young or stunted, of course)
. leaf generally serrate, <b>prominently veined abaxially</b>
. acorn cap bristled or spiked
. frequently found in redwood forests
. common

==bush tanoak:N. densiflorus var. echinoides
. shrub up to 10 feet tall
. leaf entire to few-toothed, <b>obscurely veined abaxially</b>
. <b>not present in the bay area</b>
