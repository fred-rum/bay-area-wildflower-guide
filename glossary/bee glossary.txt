<h2>Body parts</h2>

{sternite,sternites,S1--S6,S1,S2,S3,S4,S5,S6} A ventral sclerite covering one segment of the abdomen.  Bee sternites are labeled S1 from the anterior end (closest to the thorax) to S6 at the tail.

{tergite,tergites,T1--T6,T1,T2,T3,T4,T5,T6} A dorsal sclerite covering one segment of the abdomen.  Bee tergites are labelled from T1 at the anterior end (closest to the thorax) to T6 at the tail.
