<h3>head</h3>

{head,heads} The frontmost of the three major sections of the body, including the eyes, mouth, antennae, etc.  Part I in the [figure:fly side].

<h3>thorax</h3>

{thorax,thoraxes,thoraces} The middle of the three major sections of the body.  Part II in the [figure:fly side].

<h3>abdomen</h3>

{abdomen,abdomens} The hindmost of the three major sections of the body.  Part III in the [figure:fly side].

{sclerite,sclerites} A plate forming one section of an exoskeleton.


<h2>Relative positions</h2>

{anterior} Closer to the head end of the body.

{dorsal,dorsally} Related to the back of the animal or closer to the back.  Opposite ventral.

{posterior} Closer to the tail end of the body.

{ventral,ventrally} Related to the belly of the animal or closer to the belly.  Opposite dorsal.
