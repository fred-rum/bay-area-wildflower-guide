<h3>Head</h3>

{head,heads} The frontmost of the three major sections of the body, including the eyes, mouth, antennae, etc.  Part A in the figures: [figure:true bug top,top] [figure:true bug side,side].


<h3>Thorax</h3>

{thorax,thoraxes,thoraces} The middle of the three major sections of the body.  Part B in the figures: [figure:true bug top,top] [figure:true bug side,side].
{pronotum,pronotums} The dorsal sclerite covering the prothorax.  Part 25 in the figures: [figure:true bug top,top] [figure:true bug side,side].
{prothorax,prothoraxes,prothoraces} The anterior portion of the thorax, supporting the first pair of legs.

{scutellum,scutellums} The dorsal sclerite covering the center of the body posterior to the pronotum.  Part 26 in the figures: [figure:true bug top,top] [figure:true bug side,side].


<h3>Abdomen</h3>

{abdomen,abdomens} The hindmost of the three major sections of the body.  Part C in the figures: [figure:true bug top,top] [figure:true bug side,side].
