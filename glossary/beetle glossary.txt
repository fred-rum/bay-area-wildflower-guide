<h2>Body parts</h2>

{head,heads} The frontmost of the three major sections of the body, including the eyes, mouth, antennae, etc.  Part A in the [figure:beetle].

{thorax,thoraxes,thoraces} The middle of the three major sections of the body.  Part B in the [figure:beetle].
{pronotum,pronotums} The dorsal sclerite covering the prothorax.
{prothorax,prothoraxes,prothoraces} The anterior portion of the thorax, supporting the first pair of legs.  Part 9 in the [figure:beetle].

{abdomen,abdomens} The hindmost of the three major sections of the body.  Part C in the [figure:beetle].

{elytron,elytra} A wing case (wing cover).  On other insects, this would be a forewing.
