Each page in the BAWG typically describes one taxon, i.e. one group of
related organisms.  In the tree of life, this may be a leaf taxon, the
lowest level of categorization, or it may a root or branch taxon, with
any number sub-taxons below it.

Click anywhere in the example taxon page below to get more information on that section, or browse the list of sections below.
