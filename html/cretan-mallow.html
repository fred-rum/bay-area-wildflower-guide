cretan mallow

Malva multiflora

Member of mallows (genus Malva)
mallow family (family Malvaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple, pink in flowering plants

  • petals 10–16 mm, generally with 3 dark veins
    • petals are not dark at the base
  • calyx ± 4 mm
  • epicalyx < calyx
