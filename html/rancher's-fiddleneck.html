Member of common fiddleneck (Amsinckia menziesii)
fiddlenecks (genus Amsinckia)
borage family (family Boraginaceae)
borages (order Boraginales)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
orange, yellow in flowering plants

  • corolla radial, tube straight, limb with 0 or 5 dark spots
  • stamens generally attached above middle of corolla tube, if below then style exserted
  • stem ascending to erect
  • corolla 7–11 mm, limb 4–10 mm diam, ± orange, tube exserted
  • very common
