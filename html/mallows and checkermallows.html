checkerblooms and checkermallows
genus Sidalcea
  • Jepson method: bractlets subtending calyx 0(1–3), generally not forming involucel (secondary involucre)
    • but bractlets are impossible to distinguish from reduced leaves
  • my method: petals have numerous lighter veins that branch or network along their length and may fade out toward the petal tip
