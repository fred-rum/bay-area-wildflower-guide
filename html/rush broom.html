rush broom

Acmispon junceus
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to deervetch (Acmispon spp.)

  • perennial herb; prostrate or low-ascending, not woody, 8–40 cm
  • 3–5 leaflets, 5–10 mm, glabrous or finely strigose
  • calyx lobes short, wide, not hooked
  • corolla yellow, in clusters of 2–8, possible pediceled
  • scattered in the Santa Cruz range
