Gymnoconia peckiana

Member of rust fungi (order Pucciniales)
basidiomycete fungi (phylum Basidiomycota)
fungi including lichens (kingdom Fungi)

Caution: There may be other wild species of this genus not yet included in this guide.

  • The Plant Disease Diagnostic Clinic at Cornell says that this species should be split across two genera, but is unfortunately vague about the hosts for each.
    • Arthuriomyces peckianus infects “black raspberry”.
    • Gymnoconia nitens is “more common” on “blackberry”.
