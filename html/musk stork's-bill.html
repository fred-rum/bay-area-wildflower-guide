musk stork’s-bill

Erodium moschatum
Member of geranium family (family Geraniaceae)
purple in flowering plants

Key to stork’s-bills (Erodium spp.)

  • stem short hairy
  • sepals 6–9 mm, tip glabrous [with knob, but no bristles]
  • petals 10–15 mm [often shorter]
  • leaf compound; leaflets lobed to shallowly divided
  • pedicel is often a green purple, but is often short and hidden by the flower
  • flowers in an umbel
