field mustard

Brassica rapa

Member of cabbages and mustards (genus Brassica)
yellow mustards
mustard family (family Brassicaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
yellow in flowering plants

  • see yellow mustards for a key to common mustards
  • stem 30–100 cm, simple or branched
  • basal leaves pinnately lobed, lateral lobes 2–4(6) pairs, terminal lobe obovate, wavy-dentate
  • middle and distal cauline leaves sessile, base lobed, generally clasping
  • pedicel erect
  • petals 6–11(13) mm
  • very common
