chamomiles

genus Anthemis
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to white daisies

There are no other wild species of this genus in California.

 stinking chamomile
Anthemis cotula
stinking chamomile
Anthemis cotula
  • strongly scented
  • stem dotted with resin glands
  • leaf 15–30 mm wide, 1–3-pinnately divided into thread-like lobes
  • common

corn chamomile
Anthemis arvensis

  • not notably scented
  • leaf 8–16 mm wide, 1–2-pinnately lobed into linear or wider lobes [although they look pretty narrow in CalPhotos]
  • rare at Quicksilver

See also german chamomile (Matricaria chamomilla).
