pacific coast tick

Dermacentor occidentalis

Key to dog ticks (genus Dermacentor)

  • basis capitulum [dorsal plate between head and body] longer than wide
  • cornua [just aft of basis capitulum] as long as or longer than wide
  • sizes of shield punctations not greatly disparate
  • pearl grey coloration of shield more extensive than brown
