small-flowered crane’s-bill

Geranium pusillum

Member of geraniums and cranesbills (genus Geranium)
geranium family (family Geraniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • stem hairs soft
  • leaf blade 1.5–4.8 cm wide, divided 30–75% to base
  • 7 leaf segments, wedge-shaped
  • petals 2–3 mm, ± notched, pink to violet
  • 5 fertile stamens (outer 5 stamens lacking anthers) [all others have 10 fertile stamens with anthers]
  • anthers dark purple
  • stigma lobes beige

Chris’s observations: none
