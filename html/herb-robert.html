Geranium robertianum

Member of geraniums and cranesbills (genus Geranium)
geranium family (family Geraniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple in flowering plants

  • stem hairs ± sparse
  • leaf blade 3.5–10 cm wide, divided > 90% to base or midrib
  • 5 leaf segments, ± sessile to short-stalked, pinnately lobed to dissected
  • pedicel 5–20 mm
  • petals 10–14 mm, rounded, pink to red-purple [often shorter]
  • anthers ± purple [sometimes orange?]
  • stigma lobes rose to purple
