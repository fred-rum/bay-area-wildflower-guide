Member of borage family (family Boraginaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to heliotrope (genus Heliotropium)

  • stem ascending to erect, puberulent to short-soft-hairy
  • calyx lobes bristly
  • corolla white
  • rare along the Pacific coast
