musk monkeyflower

Erythranthe moschata

Member of stalked monkeyflowers (genus Erythranthe)
monkeyflowers (family Phrymaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
yellow in flowering plants

  • stolons or rhizomes present
  • leaf blade 10–40 mm
  • fruiting pedicels 4–25 mm
  • calyx lobes 2–4 mm
  • herbage glandular
  • corolla tube 2–4 mm wide except wider at mouth
  • calyx glabrous to slimy-hairy
  • leaf surfaces feel wet & slimy
  • latest refinements have these only in Marin

Chris’s observations: 1 (research grade)

Locations:

  • Desolation Wilderness

Months:

  • Aug.: 1
