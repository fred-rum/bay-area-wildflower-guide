crevice alumroot

Heuchera micrantha

Member of alumroots (genus Heuchera)
saxifrage family (family Saxifragaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
white, other colors in flowering plants

  • hypanthium + calyx 1.5–3 mm
  • leaf blade 2–10 cm wide
  • common

Compare to oneleaf foamflower (Tiarella trifoliata var. unifoliata), which has a single inflorescence axis with only short forking offshoots.
