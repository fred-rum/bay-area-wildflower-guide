pink woodland star

Lithophragma parviflorum var. parviflorum
Member of saxifrage family (family Saxifragaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to woodland stars (Lithophragma spp.)

There are no other wild members of this species in the bay area.

  • basal leaves lobed > ¾ to base to 3-parted
  • most proximal cauline leaf 3-parted
  • petals white or pink
  • very rare

Chris’s observations: none
