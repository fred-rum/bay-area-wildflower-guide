golden yarrow

Eriophyllum confertiflorum var. confertiflorum

Member of golden yarrow (Eriophyllum confertiflorum)
woolly sunflowers (genus Eriophyllum)
tarweeds and allies (tribe Madieae)
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
orange, yellow in flowering plants

There are no other wild members of this species in the bay area.

  • subshrub or shrub, generally 20–70 cm
  • 4–7 phyllaries, strongly overlapping
  • peduncle 0–2.5(9) cm
  • 4–6 (0–8) rays, 2–5 mm
  • found in many dry habitats
