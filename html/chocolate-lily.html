chocolate lily

Fritillaria biflora

Member of fritillaries (genus Fritillaria)
true lily subfamily (subfamily Lilioideae)
lily family (family Liliaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • nectary a prominent narrow band 2/3 to = perianth
  • perianth dark brown, green-purple or yellow-green
  • flower ill-scented or not
  • rare in San Mateo County

Toxicity of checker lily, fritillaria (Fritillaria spp.):
0 – Non-toxic.
