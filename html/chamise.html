common chamise
Adenostoma fasciculatum var. fasciculatum

  • plant tall with erect/ascending branches
  • twigs glabrous
  • leaves 5–13 mm long
  • found in the bay area

southern chamise
Adenostoma fasciculatum var. obtusifolium

  • plant tall with erect/ascending branches
  • twigs hairy
  • leaves 2–6.5 mm long
  • found only in southern California

carpet-forming chamise
Adenostoma fasciculatum var. prostratum

  • plant low, mounded with decumbent branches
  • found only on islands and beaches of southern California
