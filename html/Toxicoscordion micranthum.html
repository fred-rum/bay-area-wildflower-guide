smallflower deathcamas

Toxicoscordion micranthum
Member of trillium family (family Melanthiaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to deathcamas (genus Toxicoscordion)

  • petals 3–8 mm
  • stamens ± = perianth
  • pedicels in fruit 12–40 mm, generally spreading (60–90° from axis), with tips upturned or not
  • plant scabrous below
  • extremely rare

Chris’s observations: none
