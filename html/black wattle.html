black wattle

Acacia mearnsii
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
cream, other colors in flowering plants

Key to wattles (Acacia spp.)

  • 2-pinnate leaves
  • 7–31 pairs of 1st-order leaflets, 15–70 mm
  • 20–70 pairs of 2nd-order leaflets, 1.5–3.5 mm
  • leaf main axis with raised glands at most pairs of 1st-order leaflets and generally between
  • twig angled (not winged)
  • waif
