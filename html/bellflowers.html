eastwood’s bellflower
Campanula angustiflora

  • annual
  • leaf oblong-ovate
  • corolla 2–6 mm

griffin’s bellflower
Campanula griffinii

  • annual
  • leaf ± linear, leathery, serrate
  • corolla 2–4 mm
  • Jasper Ridge, Mines Rd.

chaparral harebell
Campanula exigua

  • annual
  • leaf ± linear, leathery, few-toothed
  • corolla 7–18 mm
  • uncommon in the Diablo range

sharsmith’s harebell
Campanula sharsmithiae

  • annual
  • leaf widely lanceolate, fleshy, serrate
  • corolla 7–16 mm
  • very rare

swamp harebell
Campanula californica

  • perennial
  • leaf ovate, thin, crenate
  • corolla 8–15 mm
  • extremely rare
