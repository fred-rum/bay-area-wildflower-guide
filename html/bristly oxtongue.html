bristly oxtongue

Helminthotheca echioides
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
yellow in flowering plants

Key to dandelion-like flowers

There are no other wild species of this genus in California.

  • coarse, stout, branched stem
  • basal and cauline leaves warty; proximal tapered to winged petioles; distal sessile, sometimes clasping
  • phyllaries in 2–3 overlapping series; outer phyllaries spreading to ascending
  • corolla yellow, may be ± red abaxially
