purple woodsorrel

Oxalis purpurea
Member of woodsorrel family (family Oxalidaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to woodsorrels (Oxalis spp.)

CalPhotos
  • stem ± 0
  • leaf: < 8, in basal rosette; petiole < 5 cm
  • leaflets < 4 cm, sparsely hairy or more densely hairy abaxially and on margins
  • inflorescence: peduncle 1–11 cm; bracts 2, at or below middle
  • sepals < 1 cm, ± lanceolate, hairy
  • petals < 2.5 cm, purple to red, pink, or white [yellow]
  • Nisene Marks, Montara Mtn.
