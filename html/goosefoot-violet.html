goosefoot violet

Viola purpurea

Member of violets (genus Viola)
violet family (family Violaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • leaves simple, basal and cauline
  • 1–6 basal leaves per caudex [subterranean woody base], petioles winged
  • stems prostrate to erect, with leaves, peduncles
  • cleistogamous flower present
  • scattered

Toxicity:
except for seeds of violas (Viola spp.):

0 – Non-toxic.

Chris’s observations: none
