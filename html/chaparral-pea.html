chaparral pea

Pickeringia montana

Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple, pink in flowering plants

There are no other wild species of this genus anywhere.

This species has subspecies or variants that don't seem worth distinguishing.

Leaves folded lengthwise, unlike sweet peas and vetchlings (genus Lathyrus).
