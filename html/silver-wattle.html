silver wattle

Acacia dealbata

Member of wattles (genus Acacia)
legume family (family Fabaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
cream, other colors in flowering plants

  • 2-pinnate leaves
  • 6–30 pairs of 1st-order leaflets, 15–55 mm
  • 15–70 pairs of 2nd-order leaflets, 2–5 mm
  • leaf main axis with raised glands at each pair of 1st-order leaflets
  • twig angled (not winged)
  • common
