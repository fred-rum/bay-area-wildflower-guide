There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

california yampah
Perideridia californica
  • roots tuberous
  • basal petiole 2–6 cm

gairdner’s yampah
Perideridia gairdneri

  • roots tuberous
  • basal petiole 8–15 cm
  • cauline leaves 1–2-pinnate or 1–2-ternate [not ternate-pinnate?]

oregon yampah
Perideridia oregana

  • roots tuberous
  • basal petiole 2–10 cm
  • all leaf segments alike in size and form
