oak gooseberry
Ribes quercetorum

  • five sepals
  • yellow/cream sepals and petals
  • anther filaments not longer than petals
    • all others with five sepals have anther filaments longer than petals
  • rare in Diablo range

straggly gooseberry
Ribes divaricatum var. pubiflorum

  • styles hairy at least at base [figure]
    • all others in this box have styles that are glabrous at base
  • purple to green sepals
  • white petals
  • green to cream anthers
  • leaf blade not glandular
  • internodes bristly or not
  • fruit glabrous (not bristly)

bitter gooseberry
Ribes amarum

  • purple-red sepals
  • white petals
  • purple or yellow-green anthers
  • styles glabrous at base
  • leaf blade abaxially glandular
  • no bristles between nodes
  • rare in Diablo range

hillside gooseberry
Ribes californicum
  • green or purple-red sepals
  • white petals
  • green to yellow anthers
  • styles glabrous at base
  • leaf blade abaxially nonglandular
  • young stems generally have no bristles between nodes
    • proximal stem may have dense bristles
