brittleleaf manzanita

Arctostaphylos crustacea

Member of heath family (family Ericaceae)
white in flowering plants

Key to manzanitas (genus Arctostaphylos)

There are other wild members of this species not yet included in this guide.

  • plant erect, 1–3 m
  • burl prominent at base of main stem
  • leaf:
    • adaxially dark green, glabrous
    • abaxially light green, glabrous to densely short-hairy
  • common, mostly in the Santa Cruz Range, but also found in the Diablo Range
  • 7’
