western solomon’s plume

Maianthemum racemosum ssp. amplexicaule

Member of solomon’s plume (Maianthemum racemosum)
false solomon’s seals (genus Maianthemum)
dragon tree subfamily (subfamily Nolinoideae)
asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
white in flowering plants

  • the only subspecies in California
  • stem erect [?]
  • leaf sessile, clasping
    • blade base rounded
    • tip of third leaf below inflorescence acute

Edibility: The uniformly ruby-colored ripe berries have a nice bitter-sweet flavor. The pulp is sucked off the hard seed. The fruit can last a long time while staying edible. [source] [source]
