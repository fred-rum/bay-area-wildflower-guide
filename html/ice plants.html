ice plants

Member of flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to stone plant family (family Aizoaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

slender iceplant
Mesembryanthemum nodiflorum
  • 0.4–1.2 cm flower diameter
  • leaf ± cylindric
  • white petals, aging to yellow
  • found mostly on the levees around the salt ponds at the south end of the bay
  • common

crystalline ice plant
Mesembryanthemum crystallinum

  • 1–3 cm flower diameter
  • leaf flat
  • white petals, aging to pink
  • rare
