narrow-leaved cattail

Typha angustifolia
Member of bulrush family (family Typhaceae)

Key to cattails (Typha spp.)

CalPhotos
  • naked stem between staminate, pistillate flowers 1–12 cm
  • widest leaf blades 4–15 mm wide (fresh), 3–8 mm (dry)
  • leaf blade glands 0
  • pistillate spike dark brown
  • pistillate bractlet tip dark brown, generally darker than stigma, blunt
