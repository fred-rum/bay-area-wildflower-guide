broadleaf forget-me-not

Myosotis latifolia
Member of borage family (family Boraginaceae)
pale blue in flowering plants

Key to forget-me-nots (genus Myosotis)

  • calyx tube hairs at least some spreading, some or all hooked
  • corolla 5–10 mm diameter, blue (but pink when young)
  • leaves roughly 2–3x wider than corollas, roughly 2–3x longer than wide
