orchid family (family Orchidaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
pale purple, other colors in flowering plants

  • sepals ± green, often purple-tinged or -striped
  • lateral petals 8–11 mm; lateral sepals 10–13 mm
  • ± dry places
  • very common in the Santa Cruz range and Oakland hills
