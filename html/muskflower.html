Erythranthe moschata
Member of monkeyflowers (family Phrymaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to genus Erythranthe

  • stolons or rhizomes present
  • leaf blade 20–50 mm
  • herbage glandular
  • corolla tube 2–4 mm wide except wider at mouth
  • calyx glabrous to slimy-hairy
