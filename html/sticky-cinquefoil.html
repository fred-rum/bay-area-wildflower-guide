sticky cinquefoil

Drymocallis glandulosa

Member of strawberry subtribe (subtribe Fragariinae)
rose family (family Rosaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
cream, white in flowering plants

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

Hosts sticky cinquefoil galls.

There are variants of this species, but they have overlapping characteristics, and without common names they’re just not very interesting.
