nettles

genus Urtica
Member of nettle family (family Urticaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in California.

 stinging nettle
Urtica dioica
stinging nettle
Urtica dioica
  • 50–300 cm tall
  • leaf blade 6–20 cm
  • inflorescence raceme- or panicle-like, generally > petiole

dwarf nettle
Urtica urens

  • 10–60(90) cm tall
  • leaf blade 1.8–4(6) cm
  • inflorescence generally ± head- or spike-like, often < petiole
