Member of coyote mint (Monardella villosa)
mint family (family Lamiaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple, pale purple in flowering plants

  • leaf ovate to widely triangular-ovate [ovate base with triangular tip], base generally truncate
    • in the bay area, coastal variants have thick leaves, woolly stems, while inland variants are less hairy and have smaller leaves
