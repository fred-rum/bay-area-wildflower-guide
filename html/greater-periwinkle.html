Vinca major

Member of periwinkles (genus Vinca)
european periwinkles (subtribe Vincinae)
dogbane family (family Apocynaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple in flowering plants

  • corolla 3–5 cm wide
  • leaf blade ± 7 cm
    • leaf blade base is broader than the tip
  • common throughout the bay area

Toxicity of blue buttons, vinca major (Vinca major):
4 – Ingestion of these plants, especially in large amounts, is expected to cause serious effects to the heart, liver, kidneys or brain. If ingested in any amount, call the poison center immediately.
