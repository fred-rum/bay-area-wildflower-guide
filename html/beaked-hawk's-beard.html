beaked hawk’s-beard

Crepis vesicaria ssp. taraxacifolia

There are no other wild members of this species in California.

  • basal and cauline leaves pinnately lobed to dentate, terminal lobe large, toothed
  • involucre 8–12 mm, cylindric to bell-shaped or ± obconic
  • outer phyllaries 3–4 mm, ovate to lance-linear (thick based), glabrous
  • inner phyllaries lanceolate, reflexed at maturity, often stalked-glandular
  • corolla yellow, ± red abaxially
