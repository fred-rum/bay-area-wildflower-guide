bur clover

Medicago polymorpha
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
yellow in flowering plants

Key to medicks (genus Medicago)

  • generally glabrous
  • stem prostrate, mat-forming, or ascending
  • leaflet without dark central spot, length 1–2 × width
  • stipules generally cut ± ≥ ½ width
  • corolla 3.5–6 mm, yellow
  • very common
