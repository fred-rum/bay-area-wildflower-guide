hasse vetch

Vicia hassei

Member of vetches (genus Vicia)
vetches vs. vetchlings
legume family (family Fabaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • 1–3 flowers, separated from each other
  • 4–10 leaflets, 5–25 mm, narrowly oblong to elliptic
  • leaflet tip generally truncate, notched, or 1–5-toothed [CalPhotos indicates that this may be tough to see]
  • corolla 3–9 mm, lavender to white
  • stem decumbent to erect, branched at base, 20–70 cm
