coyote brush bud gall midge
Rhopalomyia californica
  • forms in place of buds on coyote brush (Baccharis pilularis)
  • a lumpy gall up to 20 mm wide at the tip of a branch
  • often has leaves growing through the gall
 coyote brush twisted stem gall midge
Rhopalomyia baccharis
coyote brush twisted stem gall midge
Rhopalomyia baccharis
  • forms within branches of coyote brush (Baccharis pilularis)
  • a swelling and slight bending of the twig
  • adults look the same as coyote brush bud gall midge (Rhopalomyia californica), but galls and pupae are different
