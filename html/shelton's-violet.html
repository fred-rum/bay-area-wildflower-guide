shelton’s violet

Viola sheltonii

Member of violets (genus Viola)
violet family (family Violaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • Leaves ternate-compound
  • leaf blades wider than long; leaf segments 3, 2–10 mm wide
  • lateral 2 petals ± bearded or not with club-shaped hairs
  • Mt. Diablo, Ohlone

Toxicity:
except for seeds of violas (Viola spp.):

0 – Non-toxic.

Chris’s observations: none
