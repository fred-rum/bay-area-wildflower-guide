western ladyfern

Athyrium filix-femina ssp. cyclosorum

Key to ferns (class Polypodiopsida)

Member of athyrium sect. athyrium (section Athyrium)
eupolypods ii (suborder Aspleniinae)
leptosporangiate ferns (subclass Polypodiidae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

  • frond blade elliptic to lanceolate
  • lower 2–4 pinna pairs generally ≪ those above
  • midribs below near base often with minute, branched hairs

Chris’s observations: 3 (none are research grade)

Locations:

  • El Corte de Madera
  • Purisima Creek
  • Redwood Regional
