galls

Taxons that create galls are listed in their normal taxonomic locations, but I’ve also collected them here for easier browsing.

The most popular references for galls is Ron Russo’s Field Guide to Plant Galls of California (2007) and his Field Guide to Plant Galls of California and Other Western States (2021).
A good online reference is Joyce Gross’s web pages for oak calls and non-oak galls.
The Bioblitz Club also has PDF brochures for spring galls and fall galls.
Nancy Asquith has tons of additional links on iNaturalist.
