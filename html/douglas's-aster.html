douglas’s aster

Symphyotrichum subspicatum

Key to american asters (genus Symphyotrichum)

Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • stem ± hairy distally
  • outer phyllaries pale-margined > ½ length, length generally ≥ 3 × width, ± acute
  • disk flowers: yellow
  • ray flowers: 8–16 mm, violet
  • very rare

Chris’s observations: none
