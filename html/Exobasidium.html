genus Exobasidium

Key to gall fungi

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

These species parasitize manzanitas (genus Arctostaphylos) and others in the heath family (family Ericaceae).

Exobasidium vaccinii
  • forms blisters on the surface of manzanitas (genus Arctostaphylos) leaves
    • blisters cause only a minor buckling of the leaf surface, unlike manzanita leafgall aphid (Tamalia coweni), which causes signifant swelling and distortion of the leaf edge.
  • may sometimes also disrupt stem growth, although I haven’t found a description of the range of forms of this damage
