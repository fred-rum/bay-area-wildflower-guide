field mouse-ear chickweed

Cerastium arvense ssp. strictum
Member of pink family (family Caryophyllaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to mouse-ear chickweeds (Cerastium spp.)

  • stems both non-flowering/mat-forming and flowering/±erect
  • petals 7.5–9 mm, 3–6 mm > sepals
  • calyx 4.2–6 mm, glandular-hairy, rarely with hairs exceeding tip
  • scarious margin of outer sepals < 0.2 mm wide
