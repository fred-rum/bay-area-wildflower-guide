ruptured twig gall wasp

Callirhytis perdens

Key to gall wasps (family Cynipidae)

Caution: There may be other wild species of this genus not yet included in this guide.

  • parasitizes black oaks (genus Quercus)
  • after the stem ruptures, the fissures turn black
    • other stem galls of black oaks don’t create black fissures
