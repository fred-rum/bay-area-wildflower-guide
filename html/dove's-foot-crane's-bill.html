Geranium molle

Member of dove’s-foot crane’s-bill complex (complex Geranium molle)
geraniums and cranesbills (genus Geranium)
geranium family (family Geraniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple in flowering plants

  • stem hairs ± sparse, soft
  • leaf blade 0.9–5.2 cm wide, divided 50–75% to base
  • 7–9 leaf segments, obtriangular
  • petals 3–10.5 mm, notched, red-purple [usually pale purple with purple veins]
  • anthers dark purple
  • stigma lobes red-purple
