santa cruz cypress

Hesperocyparis abramsiana

Key to cypresses (Hesperocyparis spp.)

  • crown symmetric, often dense, generally pyramidal in age
  • foliage bright to dark green, not glaucous
  • plant 6–10 m
  • found in the Santa Cruz mountains
