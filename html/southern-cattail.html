southern cattail

Typha domingensis

Member of cattails (genus Typha)
bulrush family (family Typhaceae)
grasses, sedges, cattails, and allies (order Poales)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

CalPhotos
  • naked stem between staminate, pistillate flowers 1–8 cm
  • widest leaf blades 6–18 mm wide (fresh), 5–15 mm (dry)
  • leaf blade gland-dotted adaxially [inner surface] near base
  • pistillate spike cinnamon to medium brown
  • pistillate bractlet tip straw-colored (to light brown), generally paler than stigma, acute to acuminate
