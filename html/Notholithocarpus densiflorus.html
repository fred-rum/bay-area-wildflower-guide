tanoak
Notholithocarpus densiflorus var. densiflorus
  • tree up to 100 feet tall (but can be young or stunted, of course)
  • leaf generally serrate, prominently veined abaxially
  • acorn cap bristled or spiked
  • frequently found in redwood forests
  • common

bush tanoak
Notholithocarpus densiflorus var. echinoides

  • shrub up to 10 feet tall
  • leaf entire to few-toothed, obscurely veined abaxially
  • not present in the bay area
