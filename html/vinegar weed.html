vinegar weed

Trichostema lanceolatum
Member of mint family (family Lamiaceae)
purple in flowering plants

Key to bluecurls (genus Trichostema)

  • corolla tube 5–10 mm, curved upward and sharply recurved near throat
    • CalPhotos show that the corolla is abaxially sparsely hairy
  • stamens 13–20 mm
  • uppermost calyx lobe generally narrower
  • leaves lanceolate to narrowly ovate
  • leaves have a strong vinegar odor
