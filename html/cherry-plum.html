cherry-plum

Prunus cerasifera

Member of plums, cherries, and allies (genus Prunus)
rose family (family Rosaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
white in flowering plants

  • 1 flower per inflorescence (rarely 2)
  • ovary, fruit glabrous
  • flowers appear before leaves (although there can be some overlap)
  • leaf crenate-serrate
  • common

Toxicity:
in general for flowering plum, ornamental flowering plum, purple leaf plum (Prunus cerasifera 'atropurpurea'):
