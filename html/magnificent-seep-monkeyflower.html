Erythranthe grandis

Member of monkeyflowers (family Phrymaceae)
yellow in flowering plants

Key to genus Erythranthe

  • stems 50–120+ cm
  • plant densely glandular-puberulent to coarse-hairy distally
  • leaf petiole 10–80 mm, blade 25–60 mm long
  • corolla tube exserted 10–15 mm beyond calyx
  • very rare
