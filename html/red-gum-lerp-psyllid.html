insects (class Insecta)
hexapods (subphylum Hexapoda)

Caution: There may be other wild species of this genus not yet included in this guide.

  • forms on leaf surfaces of river redgum (Eucalyptus camaldulensis) and forest red gum (Eucalyptus tereticornis) by preference, but also other eucalyptus (genus Eucalyptus)
  • lerps aren’t actually galls, and they can easily be brushed off the host’s surface
