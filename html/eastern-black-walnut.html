eastern black walnut

Juglans nigra

Key to walnuts (genus Juglans)

Member of walnut family (family Juglandaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • leaflets ± serrate, narrow, rounded to acute
    • abaxial vein axils with hairy tufts
  • nut shell deeply grooved, coarsely warty
  • planted/waifs

Toxicity:
1 – Skin contact with these plants can cause symptoms ranging from redness, itching, and rash to painful blisters like skin burns.
