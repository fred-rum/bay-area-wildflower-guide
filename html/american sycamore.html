american sycamore

Platanus occidentalis
Member of plane family (family Platanaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to planes (genus Platanus)

  • leaf lobes generally shallower than for western sycamore (Platanus racemosa), but it can vary among leaves on a tree and depending on condition
  • usually only a single fruit per stalk
  • extremely rare in the bay area according to CalFlora

Chris’s observations: none
