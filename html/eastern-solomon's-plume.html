eastern solomon’s plume

Maianthemum racemosum ssp. racemosum

Member of solomon’s plume (Maianthemum racemosum)
dragon tree subfamily (subfamily Nolinoideae)
asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • found only in the eastern US
  • stem arching
  • leaf with [very short] petiole
    • blade base tapered
    • third leaf below inflorescence tapering into a thin tip, 12–25 mm long
