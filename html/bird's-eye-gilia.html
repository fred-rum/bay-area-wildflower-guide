Gilia tricolor

Member of gilias (genus Gilia)
phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple in flowering plants

  • many branches from base, spreading
  • open clusters with 2–5 flowers
  • corolla 10–19 mm, blue-violet at tip, paler below
  • corolla throat yellow or orange with large purple spots, possibly merging into a ring
