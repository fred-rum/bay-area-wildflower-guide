coastal monkeyflower

Erythranthe dentata
Member of monkeyflowers (family Phrymaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to Erythranthe spp.

  • stolons or rhizomes present
  • leaf blade 15–70 mm
  • herbage non-glandular
  • corolla tube generally > 4 mm wide
  • calyx hairy
  • very rare
