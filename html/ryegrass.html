ryegrass

Lolium spp.
Member of grasses (family Poaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

 perennial ryegrass
Lolium perenne
perennial ryegrass
Lolium perenne
  • 1 glume per spikelet (2 glumes for the uppermost spikelet)
    • spikelet sits between a hollow in the stem and the glume
  • glume < rest of spikelet
  • despite the name, is an annual or at best a perennial herb

darnel
Lolium temulentum

  • 1 glume per spikelet except 2 for uppermost spikelet
  • glume ≥ rest of spikelet

tall fescue
Lolium arundinaceum

  • 2 glumes per spikelet
  • leaf blade with prominent clasping basal auricles
  • auricles hairy

meadow fescue
Lolium pratense

  • 2 glumes per spikelet
  • leaf blade with prominent clasping basal auricles
  • auricles glabrous
