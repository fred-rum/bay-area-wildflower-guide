trilliums

genus Trillium

Member of trillium family (family Melanthiaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

Key features:

  • flower stalked or not
  • color of the 6 stamens (which are quite large and linear)
