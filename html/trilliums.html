trilliums

Trillium spp.
Member of trillium family (family Melanthiaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

Key features:

  • flower stalked or not
  • color of the 6 stamens (which are quite large and linear)

pacific trillium
Trillium ovatum
  • flower is stalked
  • stamens are yellow or ± white
  • the local subspecies is ssp. ovatum
