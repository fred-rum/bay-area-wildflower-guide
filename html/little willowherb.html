little willowherb

Epilobium minutum
Member of evening primrose family (family Onagraceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to willowherbs (Epilobium spp.)

  • < 40 cm tall
  • leaf 9–25 mm, generally lanceolate to narrowly elliptic, ± glabrous; petiole 0–2 mm
  • inflorescence open
  • hypanthium 1.1–1.5 mm
  • petals 2–5 mm, white or pink
  • scattered
