pale serviceberry

Amelanchier pallida

Member of serviceberries (genus Amelanchier)
rose family (family Rosaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • proximalmost pedicels usually 12–18 mm
  • “The species is distinctive for its often broadly oval petals, relatively long proximalmost pedicels, leaves with teeth that are relatively small and few in number, and usually absent in proximal half, and much-branched habit.” [source]
  • very rare

Chris’s observations: none
