Trifolium campestre
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
yellow in flowering plants

Key to clovers (Trifolium spp.)

  • Leaflets: 3, pinnate, petioles of mid-leaves > leaflets
  • Inflorescence: head-like, 8–13 mm wide, ovate to spheric, generally > 20-flowered; flowers quickly reflexed
  • Corolla: 4–5.5 mm, bright yellow, brown in age, striate
