Leptosiphon bicolor

Member of baby stars, linanthus, and leptosiphons (genus Leptosiphon)
phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple in flowering plants

  • corolla:
    • tube ± red
    • throat yellow
    • lobes pink/pale yellow/white
    • lobes generally < 4 mm
    • stigmas ≤ 1 mm, weakly exserted from corolla tube
      • often, but not always, shorter than the stamens
  • uncommon
