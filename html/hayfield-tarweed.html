woodrush tarweed
Hemizonia congesta ssp. luzulifolia
  • leaves short-hairy, ± shaggy, or silky-hairy
    • hairs at margins not notably longer on distal leaves
  • heads in panicle-like cluster
  • phyllaries 3.5–6.5 mm long, with tips generally shorter than bodies
  • rays white

congested-headed hayfield tarplant
Hemizonia congesta ssp. congesta

  • leaves ± shaggy
    • hairs at margins often longer on distal leaves
  • some or all heads generally in tight groups
  • phyllaries 6–10 mm long, with tips longer than bodies
  • rays white

yellow hayfield tarplant
Hemizonia congesta ssp. lutescens

  • rays yellow
