red sand spurrey

Spergularia rubra

Member of sand-spurries (genus Spergularia)
pink family (family Caryophyllaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple in flowering plants

  • base of stem not woody
  • 2–4+ leaves per axillary cluster
    • leaves barely fleshy
    • stipules generally 3.5–5 mm, shiny, white, conspicuous
  • common
