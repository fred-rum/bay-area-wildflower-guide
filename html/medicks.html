spotted medick
Medicago arabica
  • hair 0 to sparse
  • stem generally sprawling
  • leaflets with dark central spot, length ± = width
  • stipules generally cut ¼–½ width
  • corolla 4–5 mm, yellow
  • uncommon

button medick
Medicago orbicularis

  • ± glabrous
  • stem spreading, mat-forming
  • stipules generally divided ± to base into a cluster of bristles
  • corolla 3–6 mm, yellow with ± dark purple spot on back of banner
  • Jepson says it’s a waif, but its been recorded around Quicksilver for 50 years now

black medick
Medicago lupulina

  • hairy or glandular-hairy
  • stem prostrate to ascending, angled
  • stipules entire or toothed
  • corolla 2–3 mm, yellow
  • uncommon

  • glabrous or puberulent
  • stem generally erect
  • stipules entire to sharply toothed
