Key to genus Baccharis

  • leaf blade 5–40 mm long, 2–15 mm wide, obovate or oblanceolate, gland-dotted [but unknown stickiness]
  • very common
  • parasitized by the coyote brush bud gall midge (Rhopalomyia californica).
