field horsetail

Equisetum arvense

Key to horsetails (Equisetum spp.)

  • sterile stem 10–60 cm tall, green, regularly branched
    • basal internode of branch > subtending sheath [but maybe not by a lot]
    • branch is solid, has 3–4 rounded ridges lengthwise
    • sheath teeth 6–14
  • fertile stem 11–32 cm tall, brown, unbranched, fleshy
    • sheath teeth 6–10
  • common

Chris’s observations: none
