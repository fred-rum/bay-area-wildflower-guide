bee glossary

Body parts

S1–S6
One of the sternites. Sternites are labelled from S1 at the anterior end (closest to the thorax) to S6 at the tail.
sternite
A ventral sclerite covering one segment of the abdomen. Bee sternites are labeled S1 from the anterior end (closest to the thorax) to S6 at the tail.
T1–T6
One of the tergites. Tergites are labelled from T1 at the anterior end (closest to the thorax) to T6 at the tail.
tergite
A dorsal sclerite covering one segment of the abdomen.
