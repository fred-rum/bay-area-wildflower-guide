deerweed

Acmispon glaber

Key to deervetch (genus Acmispon)

Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
yellow in flowering plants

  • subshrub, erect or ascending, woody (but green), bushy branched, 50–200 cm
    • all others are not at all woody
  • calyx lobes 1–2 mm, narrow or wide
  • 3–6 leaflets (generally 3 on upper stem), 6–15 mm, glabrous or finely strigose
  • corolla yellow (reddish with age), in sessile clusters of 2–7
  • very common
