sun cup

Taraxia ovata

Member of evening primrose family (family Onagraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
yellow in flowering plants

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

See also suncups (genus Camissoniopsis).

The california sun cup (Camissoniopsis bistorta) is extremely rare in the bay area, and looks different enough to be recognizable if seen.
