western lily of the valley

Maianthemum dilatatum

Member of mayflowers (section Maianthemum)
false solomon’s seals (genus Maianthemum)
dragon tree subfamily (subfamily Nolinoideae)
asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • perianth parts 4
  • inflorescence is a raceme
  • 10–50 flowers per inflorescence
  • 2–3 leaves, 5–20 cm, cordate to sagittate; petioles 5–15 cm
  • San Bruno, Marin, Mt. Tam

Edibility: The ripe berries are either poisonous [source] or edible but fairly tasteless [source] [source].

Chris’s observations: none
