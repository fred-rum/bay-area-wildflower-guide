coastal gum plant
Grindelia stricta var. platyphylla

  • up to 100 cm tall
  • herb; stems woody for ≤ 10 cm at base of plant
  • phyllaries generally spreading, recurved, or coiled, round in cross section
  • leaves generally sessile, sometimes clasping
  • found in all coastal biomes

marsh gumplant
Grindelia stricta var. angustifolia

  • 100–200 cm tall
  • subshrub; stems woody for 30–150 cm at base of plant
  • phyllaries appressed except for erect tip < 3 mm, flat in cross section
  • leaves at least sometimes clasping
  • found in tidal wetlands
