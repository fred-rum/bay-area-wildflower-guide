purua grass

(clubrush)
genus Bolboschoenus
Member of sedges (family Cyperaceae)
cream, other colors in flowering plants

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

Plant is tipped with a cluster of spikelets, subtended by long leaves. Each spikelet has multiple interweaved flowers, the structure of which is unclear to me. Each flower has 3 stamens and 1 style with 2 or 3 stigmas.

saltmarsh clubrush
Bolboschoenus maritimus ssp. paludosus

  • 2 stigmas per flower
  • fruit 2-sided
  • anthers yellow

seacoast bulrush
Bolboschoenus robustus

  • 3 stigmas per flower
  • fruit flat-3-sided or 2-sided
  • anthers orange

river bulrush
Bolboschoenus fluviatilis

  • 3 stigmas per flower
  • fruit strongly 3-sided
  • anthers yellow
