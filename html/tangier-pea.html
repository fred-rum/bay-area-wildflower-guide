tangier pea

Lathyrus tingitanus
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to sweet peas and vetchlings (genus Lathyrus)

  • 2–3 flowers in inflorescence
  • stem with wings < 2 mm wide
  • 2 leaflets, 2–6 cm, lanceolate to widely ovate
  • corolla 25–30 mm, salmon to red-purple
  • calyx tube > lobes
