sticky sand-spurrey

Spergularia macrotheca
Member of pink family (family Caryophyllaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to sand-spurries (genus Spergularia)

There are no other wild members of this species anywhere.

Key features:

  • petal color
  • exact style length

There are three yellow styles clustered on top of what I believe is the wider, 3-lobed yellow ovary. The styles separate near the top, where the stigma for each is. It is unclear how much of the separated portion is style vs. stigma.
