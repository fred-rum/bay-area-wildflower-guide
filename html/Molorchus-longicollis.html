Molorchus longicollis

Key to longhorn beetles (family Cerambycidae)

Caution: There may be other wild species of this genus not yet included in this guide.

Per Ken-ichi Ueda’s observation, there are only two species of Molorchus in California, and M. eburneus can be distinguished by the white markings on its elytra.
