oak gooseberry

Ribes quercetorum
Member of gooseberry family (family Grossulariaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to gooseberries

  • five sepals
  • yellow/cream sepals and petals
  • anther filaments not longer than petals
    • all others with five sepals have anther filaments longer than petals
  • rare in Diablo range

Chris’s observations: none
