Clarkia rubicunda

Member of clarkia (genus Clarkia)
evening primrose family (family Onagraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
red purple in flowering plants

  • all 4 sepals staying fused
  • ovary and immature fruit 4-grooved
  • petals with ± red spot or zone at base of petal
  • petals 10–30 mm
  • stigma exserted beyond anthers
  • found everywhere

Toxicity of clarkia, farewell to spring, jodetia, godetia (Clarkia spp., Godetia spp.):
0 – Non-toxic.
