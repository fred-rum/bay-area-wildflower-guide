western fence lizard

Sceloporus occidentalis

Key to spiny lizards (genus Sceloporus)

This species has subspecies or variants that don't seem worth distinguishing.

  • can appear nearly black when cold
  • longer and thicker spines on scales
  • common
  • From California Herps: “Researchers are finding that the traditional taxonomy of Sceloporus occidentalis is incorrect, therefore many herpetologists do not currently break the species down into subspecies.”
  • traditionally, all local subspecies are coast range fence lizards, ssp. bocourtii.
