purple sack clover
Trifolium depauperatum var. truncatum

pale sack clover
Trifolium depauperatum var. amplectens

  • involucre bracts ± free or partially fused
  • no more than a pale blush of red-purple color?
    • see also bull clover (Trifolium fucatum)

dwarf sack clover
Trifolium depauperatum var. depauperatum

  • no involucre bracts
    • not to be confused with the calyx lobes of individual flowers
