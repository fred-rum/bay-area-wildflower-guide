goldenbushes

Ericameria spp.
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Except for old historical records and extremely rare examples that I don't expect to encounter, there are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

 golden-fleece
Ericameria arborescens
golden-fleece
Ericameria arborescens
  • phyllaries unequal in a graduated series
  • no rays
  • leaves and often young stem dotted with round, ± evenly spaced, resin-filled pits
  • scattered

rubber rabbitbrush
Ericameria nauseosa

  • phyllaries unequal in a graduated series
  • no rays
  • leaves and young stem lacking round resin pits
  • rare
 california goldenbush
Ericameria ericoides
california goldenbush
Ericameria ericoides
  • phyllaries unequal in a graduated series
  • tips of outermost phyllaries generally abruptly pointed, leaf-like
  • 3–6 rays, 6–8 mm
  • rare

narrowleaf goldenbush
Ericameria linearifolia

  • phyllaries ± equal length
  • 12–18 rays, 9–20 mm
  • rare
