sand-loving wallflower

Erysimum ammophilum
Member of mustard family (family Brassicaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to wallflowers (Erysimum spp.)

  • stem base not woody
  • stem 0.4–9(13) dm
  • leaves linear-oblanceolate, 2–9 mm wide
  • flower petals yellow
  • Ecology: coastal dunes
  • rare on Pacific coast near Butano and Big Basin

Chris’s observations: none
