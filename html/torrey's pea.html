torrey’s pea

Lathyrus torreyi
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to sweet peas and vetchlings (Lathyrus spp.)

  • 1–2 flowers in inflorescence
  • 10–15 leaflets per leaf
  • leaf axis ends in bristle or not (not coiled or branched tendril)
  • corolla 8–10 mm, lilac to pale purple-blue
