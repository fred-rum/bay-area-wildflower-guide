torrey’s willowherb

Epilobium torreyi

Key to willowherbs (genus Epilobium)

Member of evening primrose family (family Onagraceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • 4–65 cm tall
  • leaf 5–45 mm, ± lance-linear, hairy, ± sessile
  • bracts leaf-like
  • hypanthium 0.4–1 mm
  • petals 1–3.2 mm, pink or white
  • Jasper Ridge, Nisene Marks, Sunol, Mt. Diablo, Mt. Tam

Chris’s observations: none
