hedge parslies

Torilis spp.
Member of carrot family (family Apiaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

Note that references to “umbel” below refer to one umbel in the compound umbel inflorescence.

 common hedge parsley
Torilis arvensis
common hedge parsley
Torilis arvensis
  • plant erect
  • umbel open, not head-like
  • umbel subtended by ± 0–1 bracts
  • common

knotted hedgeparsley
Torilis nodosa

  • plant spreading
  • umbel dense, head-like

upright hedge-parsley
Torilis japonica

  • plant erect
  • umbel open, not head-like
  • umbel subtended by ± 5–6 bracts (info source)
  • only in/around Tilden
