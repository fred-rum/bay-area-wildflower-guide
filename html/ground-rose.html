ground rose

Rosa spithamea

Member of roses (genus Rosa)
rose tribe (tribe Roseae)
rose family (family Rosaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple, red purple in flowering plants

  • dwarf shrub, generally < 50 cm
  • prickles 3–8(12) mm, generally slender (thick-based), ± straight
  • leaf axis generally glabrous (finely hairy), glandular
  • leaflets 5–7(9), (±) glabrous
  • hypanthium stalked-glandular
  • sepals generally glandular, entire
  • petals 10–15 mm, pink to red
  • common in the bay area

Toxicity:
