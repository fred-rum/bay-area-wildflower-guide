miner’s lettuce

Claytonia perfoliata

Member of claytonia sect. limnia (section Limnia)
spring beauties (genus Claytonia)
pussypaw family (family Montiaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
white in flowering plants

  • cauline leaves substantial, fully fused
  • basal leaf 1–25 cm long including petiole, blade < 4 cm, < 3× longer than wide, elliptic to reniform
  • few to many basal leaves, spreading to erect, ± equal
  • corolla white or pink

Toxicity:
0 – Non-toxic.
