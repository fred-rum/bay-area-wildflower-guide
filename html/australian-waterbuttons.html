australian waterbuttons

Cotula australis

Member of buttons (genus Cotula)
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • minutely strigose to long-soft-hairy
  • leaf blade 2–3-pinnately divided
  • corolla dull white to pale yellow, 0.5–0.8 mm
  • scattered
