pink woodland star

Lithophragma parviflorum var. parviflorum

Member of smallflower woodland star (Lithophragma parviflorum)
woodland stars (genus Lithophragma)
saxifrage family (family Saxifragaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild members of this species in the bay area.

  • basal leaves lobed > ¾ to base to 3-parted
  • most proximal cauline leaf 3-parted
  • petals white or pink
  • very rare

Chris’s observations: none
