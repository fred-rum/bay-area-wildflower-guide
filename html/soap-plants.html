genus Chlorogalum

Member of century plant subfamily (subfamily Agavoideae)
asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

These flowers open in the evening. For flowers that open during the day, see genus Hooveria.

 wavy-leafed soap plant
Chlorogalum pomeridianum
wavy-leafed soap plant
Chlorogalum pomeridianum
  • leaf 4–25 mm wide, margin generally wavy
  • common

The leaves are wider, wavier, duller, and paler green than irises (genus Iris).

narrowleaf soap plant
Chlorogalum angustifolium

  • leaf 2–5 mm wide, margin ± flat
  • extremely rare in Marin
