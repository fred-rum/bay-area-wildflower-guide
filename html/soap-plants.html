soap plants

genus Chlorogalum

Member of asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

These flowers open in the evening. For flowers that open during the day, see genus Hooveria.
