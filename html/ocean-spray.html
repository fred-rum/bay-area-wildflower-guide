Holodiscus discolor var. discolor

  • inflorescence 5–25 cm, 5–25 cm wide, branches generally many
  • leaf blade 1.5–8 cm, teeth toothed, base generally truncate to rounded
  • common

Holodiscus discolor var. glabrescens

  • inflorescence 2–8 cm, 1.5–5 cm wide, branches 0–few
  • leaf blade 0.3–3 cm, teeth entire, above middle, base generally wedge-shaped
  • leaf puberulent to glabrous both surfaces, glands visible
  • not known in the bay area

small-leaf cream bush
Holodiscus discolor var. microphyllus

  • inflorescence 2–8 cm, 1.5–5 cm wide, branches 0–few
  • leaf blade 0.3–3 cm, teeth entire, above middle, base generally wedge-shaped
  • leaf ± long-hairy 1 or both surfaces, glands 0 or generally obscured by hairs
  • not known in the bay area
