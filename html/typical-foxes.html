There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

red fox
Vulpes vulpes

  • about 7–14 pounds; longer legged than the gray fox
  • coat is entirely reddish
  • tracks have a larger gap between pads and are less distinct than coyote due to fur between toe pads
    • front and rear tracks often overlap significantly [source]

san joaquin kit fox
Vulpes macrotis ssp. mutica

  • about 3½–6 pounds with very large ears relative to its head
  • only observed east of the Diablo Range
