The dorsal sclerite covering the prothorax. [insect glossary]

The anterior portion of the thorax, supporting the first pair of legs. Part 9 in the [figure]. [insect glossary]

The hindmost of the three major sections of the body. Part C in the [figure]. [insect glossary]
elytron
A wing case (wing cover). On other insects, this would be a forewing.
