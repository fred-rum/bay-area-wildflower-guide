gall midges

subfamily Cecidomyiinae
Member of flies (order Diptera)

The definitive reference for galls is Ron Russo’s Field Guide to Plant Galls of California.
A good online reference is Joyce Gross’s web pages for oak calls and non-oak galls.
The Bioblitz Club also has PDF brochures for spring galls and fall galls.

coyote brush bud gall midge
Rhopalomyia californica
  • parasitizes coyote brush (Baccharis pilularis)
  • a lumpy gall up to 20 mm wide at the tip of a branch
  • often has leaves growing through the gall
