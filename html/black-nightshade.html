black nightshade

Solanum nigrum

Member of nightshade family (family Solanaceae)
white in flowering plants

Key to nightshades (genus Solanum)

  • infloresecnce rarely forked
    • peduncle 0.5–1.5 cm long, straight; pedicels 3–5 mm long, 0.2–0.3 mm in diameter at the base and 0.2–0.3 mm at the apex
  • corolla: 10–17 mm diameter including lobes, white w/ yellow/green spots at base
    • anthers 1.4–2.4 mm
    • if we believe CalPhotos, style may or may not be strongly exerted beyond anthers
  • leaf: 4–7 cm, entire to coarsely wavy-toothed
  • hairs: ± spreading or curved, glandular and not
  • calyx lobes partially fused, not reflexed in fruit
  • fruit: dull
    • [photographs show the sepals in fruit being strongly, weakly, or not reflexed, but Jasper Ridge says they’re never reflexed]
