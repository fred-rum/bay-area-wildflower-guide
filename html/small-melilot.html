small melilot

Melilotus indicus
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
yellow in flowering plants

Key to melilots and sweetclovers (genus Melilotus)

  • corolla 2.5–3 mm, yellow
  • inflorescence axis generally 1–2 cm when flowers open
  • stem 10–60 cm
  • corolla length is ± 1½–2½ × calyx length
  • wings length ± = banner length
  • wings and banner length ± ≥ keel length
  • leaflets 1–2.5 cm, oblanceolate to wedge-shaped-obovate, ± toothed
  • fruit 2–3 mm, bumpy or with faint lines
  • common
