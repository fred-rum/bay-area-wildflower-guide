white sweetclover

Melilotus albus

Member of melilots and sweetclovers (genus Melilotus)
legume family (family Fabaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • corolla 3.5–5 mm, white
  • inflorescence axis generally 3–8 cm when flowers open
  • stem 50–200 cm
  • leaflets 1–2.5 cm, elliptic-oblong to obovate, ± toothed
  • fruit 3–5 mm, with network of lines
  • uncommon

Chris’s observations: none
