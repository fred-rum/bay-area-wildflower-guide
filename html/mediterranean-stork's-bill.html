mediterranean stork’s-bill

Erodium botrys

Member of stork’s-bills (genus Erodium)
geranium family (family Geraniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple in flowering plants

  • petals are rounder and with more prominent veins than in common/musk
  • sepals 10–13 mm, tip with one or more bristles
  • petals ± > sepals (usually hiding the sepals)
  • all five petals show three distinct stripes each
  • at least one sepal has red edges
  • leaf lobed to dissected
