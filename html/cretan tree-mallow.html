cretan tree-mallow

Malva multiflora
Member of mallow family (family Malvaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to mallows (Malva spp.)

  • petals 10–16 mm, generally with 3 dark veins
  • calyx ± 4 mm
  • bractlets widely lanceolate to roundish, bases fused to each other, < calyx
