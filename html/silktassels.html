silktassels

genus Garrya

Member of silktassel family (family Garryaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Except for old historical records and extremely rare examples that I don't expect to encounter, there are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

Key features:

  • abaxial leaf hairs
  • fruit hairs are also useful, but perhaps cannot fully distinguish species on their own

Parasitized by silktassel flower gall midge (Asphondylia garryae).
