mountain jewelflower

Streptanthus tortuosus

Member of jewelflowers (genus Streptanthus)
mustard family (family Brassicaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple, red purple, other colors in flowering plants

  • very similar to bristly jewelflower (Streptanthus glandulosus), but
    • petals not crinkled
    • raceme bracted below lowest flower or between lowest two flowers [example)
    • stem and leaves glabrous
  • very rare at the unvisited edges of the bay area

Chris’s observations: 1 (research grade)

Locations:

  • Ansel Adams Wilderness

  • Sep.: 1
