Key to tidytips (genus Layia)

Member of tarweeds and allies (tribe Madieae)
yellow daisies
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
yellow in flowering plants

  • most have only yellow; only some have white or pale yellow tips
  • glandular
  • phyllary tip (extending up the petal) much shorter than its folded base [CalPhotos shows at least one exception, but it may be misidentified]
  • stem purple-streaked, ascending, not strictly erect [dunno what this means; they look erect enough on CalPhotos]
  • proximal leaves serrate or lobed “< or > 1/2 to midvein”
  • scattered
