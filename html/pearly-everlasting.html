pearly everlasting

Anaphalis margaritacea

Member of everlastings (genus Anaphalis)
paper daisies (tribe Gnaphalieae)
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
white in flowering plants

There are no other wild species of this genus in California.

Each head is composed of only disc flowers surrounded by ± white phyllaries. The involucre is ± spheric. Compare to rabbit-tobaccos (genus Pseudognaphalium).
