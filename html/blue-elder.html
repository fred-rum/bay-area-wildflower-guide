blue elder

Sambucus cerulea

Member of elder family (family Viburnaceae)
yellow, cream, white in flowering plants

Key to elders (genus Sambucus)

There are no wild members of this species in California.

  • inflorescence ± flat-topped, central axis generally not dominant
  • petals ± spreading
  • glaucus blue berries

Formerly a subspecies of black elderberry, but now elevated to its own species.
