stinking chamomile

Anthemis cotula

Member of chamomiles (genus Anthemis)
white daisies
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
white in flowering plants

  • strongly scented
  • stem dotted with resin glands
  • leaf 15–30 mm wide, 1–3-pinnately divided into thread-like lobes
  • common

Toxicity:
1 – Skin contact with these plants can cause symptoms ranging from redness, itching, and rash to painful blisters like skin burns.
