Key to clarkia (genus Clarkia)

There are no other wild members of this species in the bay area.

Clarkia amoena ssp. huntiana

  • stem erect
  • inflorescence open
  • internode generally > subtending flower
  • very rare: Mt. Tam and Milagra Ridge

Clarkia amoena ssp. amoena

  • stem decumbent
  • inflorescence dense
  • internode generally < subtending flower
  • rare: but modern records are extremely rare

Clarkia amoena ssp. whitneyi

  • extremely rare: in Half Moon Bay, possibly cultivated
