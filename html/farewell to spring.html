farewell to spring

Clarkia amoena
Member of evening primrose family (family Onagraceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to clarkia (Clarkia spp.)

There are no other members of this species in the bay area.

Clarkia amoena ssp. huntiana

  • stem erect
  • inflorescence open
  • internode generally > subtending flower
  • very rare: Mt. Tam and Milagra Ridge

Clarkia amoena ssp. amoena

  • stem decumbent
  • inflorescence dense
  • internode generally < subtending flower
  • rare: but modern records are extremely rare

whitney’s farewell-to-spring
Clarkia amoena ssp. whitneyi

  • not in Jepson
  • extremely rare: in Half Moon Bay, possibly cultivated
