solomon’s plume

Maianthemum racemosum

Member of false solomon’s seals (genus Maianthemum)
dragon tree subfamily (subfamily Nolinoideae)
asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)
monocots (class Liliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild members of this species anywhere.

A key to subspecies can be found in Flora of North America.

western solomon’s plume
Maianthemum racemosum ssp. amplexicaule
  • the only subspecies in California
  • stem erect [?]
  • leaf sessile, clasping
    • blade base rounded
    • tip of third leaf below inflorescence acute

eastern solomon’s plume
Maianthemum racemosum ssp. racemosum

  • found only in the eastern US
  • stem arching
  • leaf with [very short] petiole
    • blade base tapered
    • third leaf below inflorescence tapering into a thin tip, 12–25 mm long
