shortpod mustard

Hirschfeldia incana
Member of mustard family (family Brassicaceae)
yellow in flowering plants

Key to mustards

There are no other species of this genus anywhere.

  • sepals spreading to reflexed [but the few existing photos indicate erect or ascending, rarely spreading, never reflexed]
  • stigma entire
  • basal leaves in a rosette
  • cauline leaves dentate to pinnately lobed, petioled or sessile
  • very common
