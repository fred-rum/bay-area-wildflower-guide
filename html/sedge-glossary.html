sedge glossary

floret
A unit of a spikelet, composed of a flower enclosed by a single scale-like bract. [Jepson]
spikelet
The rough equivalent of an inflorescence for sedge. A spikelet is one or more florets growing alternately. Note that a spikelet is not necessarily related to a spike. [Jepson]
