pacific aster

Symphyotrichum chilense
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
pale purple in flowering plants

Key to american asters (Symphyotrichum spp.)

  • stem hairy distally
  • outer phyllaries obtuse
  • disk flowers: yellow
  • ray flowers: 8–12 mm, violet
  • very common, especially in the Santa Cruz range
