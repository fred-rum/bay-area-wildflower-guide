american groundsels and ragworts

genus Packera

Member of groundsels and allies (tribe Senecioneae)
yellow daisies
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

Jepson doesn’t describe well how to distinguish these.

 brewer’s ragwort
Packera breweri
brewer’s ragwort
Packera breweri
  • generally 1 stem
  • glabrous or sparsely hairy when young
  • 15–50+ heads
  • green phyllary tips
  • phyllaries glabrous

widehead groundsel
Packera eurycephala var. eurycephala

  • 1 or several clustered stems
  • glabrous or densely tomentose
  • 5–30+ heads
  • phyllary tips sometimes yellow
  • phyllaries densely tomentose proximally (not reliably shown in in CalPhotos)
