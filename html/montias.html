small-leaved blinks
Montia parvifolia
  • basal or alternate on stolons
    • 15–60 mm long [bigger than on blinks (Montia fontana)]
  • petals 7–15 mm long
  • rare in the outer coastal range

diffuse montia
Montia diffusa

  • alternate leaves, basal and cauline
    • 8–50 mm long
  • petals 3–4.5 mm long
  • very rare in Marin

blinks
Montia fontana

  • opposite leaves, basal and cauline
    • 3–20 mm long
  • petals 1–2 mm long
  • rare everywhere
