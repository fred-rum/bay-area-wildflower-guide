slender woolly buckwheat

Eriogonum gracile

Member of wild buckwheats (genus Eriogonum)
knotweed family (family Polygonaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pink, salmon, white in flowering plants

  • flowers not at branchlet tips, generally appressed with no peduncles
  • involucres 2–3 mm
  • outer perianth lobes not fan-shaped or hastate
  • leaf blades oblong-obovate to oblanceolate or oblong
  • scattered
