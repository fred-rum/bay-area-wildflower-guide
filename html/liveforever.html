coast dudleya
Dudleya caespitosa

  • petals bright yellow
  • plant 8–32 cm wide
  • leaves 3–10 × longer than wide

lanceleaf liveforever
Dudleya lanceolata

  • petals yellow to generally red
  • plant 3–35 cm wide
  • leaves 3–10 × longer than wide
  • stem of older plants covered by dried leaves
