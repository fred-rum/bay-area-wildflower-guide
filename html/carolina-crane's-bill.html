Geranium carolinianum

Member of section geranium (section Geranium)
geraniums and cranesbills (genus Geranium)
geranium family (family Geraniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • stem hairs dense, short, spreading or reflexed
  • leaf blade 2.5–8.5 cm wide, divided 70–90% to base
  • 5(7) segments, oblong to wedge-shaped
  • petals 5.5–6 mm, ± notched, white to rose-pink
  • anthers dark purple
  • stigma lobes red-purple
