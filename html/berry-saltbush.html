Atriplex semibaccata

Member of amaranth family (family Amaranthaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to saltbushes (genus Atriplex)

  • perennial herb/subshrub, < 35 cm [tall?], generally mat-like with stems up to 100 cm long
  • leaf ± sessile to short-petioled with tapered base, entire to wavy-dentate
  • very common around the edges of the bay
