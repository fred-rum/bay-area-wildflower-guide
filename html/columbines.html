columbines

Aquilegia spp.
Member of buttercup family (family Ranunculaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

 western columbine
Aquilegia formosa
western columbine
Aquilegia formosa
  • each petal opening is ±round
  • inner petal edge ≤ 90° to exposed filaments
  • outer petal edge extended 1–8 mm to somewhat cup the filaments
  • common
 van houtte’s columbine
Aquilegia eximia
van houtte’s columbine
Aquilegia eximia
  • each petal opening is ±elongated
  • inner petal edge > 90° to exposed filaments
  • outer petal edge not extended, so filaments are fully visible from the side
  • uncommon
