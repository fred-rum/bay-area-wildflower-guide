serpentine collomia

Collomia diversifolia

Member of trumpets (genus Collomia)
phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

  • corolla petals red purple to pink, throat white and yellow, tube red or brown
    • corolla 9–12 mm long
  • stem branching and spreading with long glandular hairs
  • lower cauline leaves ±3-toothed (upper leaves entire)
  • very rare, mostly on Mt. Diablo

Chris’s observations: none
