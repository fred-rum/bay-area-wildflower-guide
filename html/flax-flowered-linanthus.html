flax-flowered linanthus

Leptosiphon liniflorus

Member of baby stars, linanthus, and leptosiphons (genus Leptosiphon)
phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple, white in flowering plants

  • calyx slightly shorter than corolla tube & throat
  • corolla:
    • throat yellow
    • lobes white, usually with distinct colored veins
    • lobes 8–10 mm
  • uncommon
