santa cruz aquatic garter snake

Thamnophis atratus ssp. atratus

Key to aquatic garter snake (Thamnophis atratus)

Member of natricine snakes (subfamily Natricinae)
colubrid snakes (family Colubridae)

  • yellow top stripe
  • faint yellow or missing side stripes
  • dark/black body with no red spots
  • Santa Cruz mountains south of Pacifica and west of Highway 17 and Santa Cruz
    • north and east in the outer coastal range have significant intergrades among multiple subspecies
