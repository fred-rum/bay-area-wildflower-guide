snapdragons

genus Sairocarpus

Member of plantain family (family Plantaginaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

wiry snapdragon
Sairocarpus vexillocalyculatus ssp. vexillocalyculatus
  • proximal-most flowers subtended by twining branchlets and/or flower branchlets
  • proximal stem glabrous or with nonglandular hairs

sierra snapdragon
Sairocarpus multiflorus

  • lowest flowers not subtended by branchlets
  • lower calyx lobes < to ≪ corolla tube
  • corolla 7–18 mm

twining snapdragon
Neogaerrhinum strictum

  • pedicels 3–9 cm, thread-like, twining or hooked
    • all others are < 2.5 cm, not twining or hooked

The remainder are quite rare in the bay area.
