currants and gooseberries

genus Ribes
Member of gooseberry family (family Grossulariaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Except for extremely rare examples that I don't expect to encounter, there are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

Key features:

  • spines on branch nodes or not
  • bristles between branch nodes or not (particularly on distal twigs)
  • sepal color, spread angle, and number
  • anther color
  • styles hairy at base or glabrous
  • stamen length relative to petals
  • leaf glandular on underside or not
