mexican fleabane

(santa barbara daisy, latin american fleabane)
Erigeron karvinskianus

Member of fleabanes and horseweeds (genus Erigeron)
white daisies
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
pale purple, white in flowering plants

  • ray 5–8 mm
  • phyllaries generally ± equal
  • no basal leaves at flower
  • cauline leaves elliptic to obovate, not at all clasping, entire or with 1–2 pairs of acute teeth or shallow lobes near tip
  • rare
