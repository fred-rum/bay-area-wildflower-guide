field monkey-flower

(villous-bracted monkeyflower)
Erythranthe arvensis

Member of seep monkeyflowers (section Simiolus)
stalked monkeyflowers (genus Erythranthe)
monkeyflowers (family Phrymaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
yellow in flowering plants

  • [very small corolla]; FNA suggests that there are both small-flowered and smaller-flowered versions (that were at one time called Mimulus micranthus)
    • these may be distinguished in the future
  • stems 5–70 cm, glabrous
  • leaf petiole 0–95 mm, blade 5–35 mm long
  • corolla lobes unequal, asymmetric when swollen in fruit
  • corolla [tube?] exserted 0–3 mm from calyx tube
  • bracts densely villous [hairy] abaxially
