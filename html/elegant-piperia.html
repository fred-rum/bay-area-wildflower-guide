elegant piperia

(coast piperia)
Platanthera elegans ssp. elegans

Key to bog orchids (genus Platanthera)

Member of elegant rein orchid (Platanthera elegans)
orchid family (family Orchidaceae)
white in flowering plants

There are no other wild members of this species in the bay area.

  • spur 7–14 mm, curved down along flower stalk
  • perianth white
