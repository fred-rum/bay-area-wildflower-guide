european red elder
Sambucus racemosa ssp. racemosa
  • red berries
  • only subspecies found in the bay area
  • POWO splits the North American var. microbotrys from the European ssp. racemosa, but iNaturalist doesn’t yet recognize var. microbotrys
  • Calflora and Jepson has var. microbotrys as a synonym of var. racemosa

rocky mountain elder
Sambucus racemosa var. melanocarpa

  • purple-black berries
  • in California is only found in the Sierras
