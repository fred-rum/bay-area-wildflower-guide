corn chamomile

Anthemis arvensis

Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to chamomiles (genus Anthemis)

  • not notably scented
  • leaf 8–16 mm wide, 1–2-pinnately lobed into linear or wider lobes [although they look pretty narrow in CalPhotos]
  • rare at Quicksilver
