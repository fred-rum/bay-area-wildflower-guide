golden chinquapin

Chrysolepis chrysophylla

Member of beech family (family Fagaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Except for historical records that I'm ignoring, there are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

There are no other wild members of this species anywhere.

Chinquapins also occur in other genuses, but this is the only genus of chinquapins in California.

Hosts chinquapin galls.
