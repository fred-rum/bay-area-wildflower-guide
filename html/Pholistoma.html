genus Pholistoma

Member of borage family (family Boraginaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in California.

 fiesta flower
Pholistoma auritum
fiesta flower
Pholistoma auritum
  • corolla 7–15 mm, 10–30 mm wide, blue to purple with darker marks in throat
  • leaf petiole wide-winged, clasping
  • leaf blade tip acuminate
 white fiesta flower
Pholistoma membranaceum
white fiesta flower
Pholistoma membranaceum
  • corolla 3–6 mm, < 10 mm wide, white, lobe generally with purple spot
  • leaf petiole narrow-winged, not clasping
  • leaf blade oblong to ovate [i.e. lobes don’t consistently taper toward the tip]
