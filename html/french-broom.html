french broom

Genista monspessulana

Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
yellow in flowering plants

Key to genus Genista

  • leaflet not narrow (length < 5 × width), margins flat
  • leaflet generally 10–15 mm
  • stipules < 2 mm, deciduous
  • racemes axillary
  • very common
