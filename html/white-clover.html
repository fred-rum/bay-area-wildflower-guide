white clover

Trifolium repens

Member of clovers (genus Trifolium)
legume family (family Fabaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
white in flowering plants

  • stem creeping, rooting, turf-forming
  • petioles growing from ground level
  • generally ± glabrous
  • Leaflets: 3, palmate, 0.5–2.5 cm, obovate
  • Bracts: generally inconspicuous
  • Infloresence: 1–2.5 cm wide
  • Calyx: 3–6 mm, lobes tapered, all or lower > tube
  • Corolla: 7–11 mm, white
