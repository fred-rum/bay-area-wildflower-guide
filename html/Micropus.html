genus Micropus

flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

Jepson has a drawing of the palea of both species, and CalPhotos has a photo of M. californicus paleae. The palea wing orientation may be easier to see when one palea is removed from the head as in these drawings.
