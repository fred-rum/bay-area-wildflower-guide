strawberries

genus Fragaria

Member of rose family (family Rosaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other wild species of this genus in the bay area.

 woodland strawberry
Fragaria vesca
woodland strawberry
Fragaria vesca
  • leaf thin, not leathery; central leaflet stalk 1–3 mm
  • petals 4–9 mm

beach strawberry
Fragaria chiloensis

  • leaf thick, leathery; central leaflet stalk 1–10 mm
  • petals 10–18 mm
  • mostly near the Pacific coast, with a few scattered around the edges of the bay
