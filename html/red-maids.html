red maids

Calandrinia menziesii

Member of genus Calandrinia
pussypaw family (family Montiaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple, red purple in flowering plants

  • petals 4–15 mm, bright pink-purple (rarely white)
  • 3–15 stamens
  • common

Red maids in California appear identical to C. ciliata in South America and used to share the same scientific name, but they are now known to be genetically different. So the ones in California are now C. menziesii. (Reference.)
