Key to vetches (Vicia spp.)

  • generally 1–3 flowers, at tip
  • peduncle 1–3 cm
  • 4–10 leaflets, 6–20 mm, linear to elliptic
  • corolla 4–6 mm, pale lavender to light purple
  • stem slender, decumbent or climbing, 10–50 cm
