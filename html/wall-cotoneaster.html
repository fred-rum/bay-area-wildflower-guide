Key to cotoneasters (genus Cotoneaster)

  • shrub 0.5–1 m
  • leaf blade blade 4–14 mm, widely elliptic; adaxially ± glabrous, shiny; abaxially sparsely appressed hairy, tip acute
  • very rare: Mt. Tam
