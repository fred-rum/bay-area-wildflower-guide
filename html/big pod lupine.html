big pod lupine

Lupinus pachylobus
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

Key to lupines (Lupinus spp.)

CalPhotos
  • habit: Annual 1.5–4 dm, hairy
  • leaf: petiole 4–8 cm; leaflets generally 7, 20–25 mm, 2–5 mm wide
  • inflorescence: 1–4 cm, flowers generally whorled; peduncle 3–12 cm; pedicels 1–2.5 mm; bract ± 6 mm
  • flower: 7–9 mm; calyx 4.5–6 mm, lips ± equal; petals blue, banner longer than wide, spot white, in age dark magenta
  • keel blunt, glabrous
  • Mt. Diablo
