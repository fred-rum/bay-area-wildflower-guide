many-stemmed california gilia

(small california gilia)
Gilia achilleifolia ssp. multicaulis

Member of california gilia (Gilia achilleifolia)
gilias (genus Gilia)
phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)
dicots (class Magnoliopsida)
purple, pale purple, white in flowering plants

  • 1–7 flowers in open cyme
    • corolla 5–10 mm long (1–2 × calyx)
    • pedicel 1–30 mm
  • when the flower is very pale, it may be mistaken for chaparral gilia (Gilia angelensis), but that doesn’t grow in the bay area
